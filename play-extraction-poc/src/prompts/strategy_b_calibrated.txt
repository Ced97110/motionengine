MOVEMENT TYPES (Basketball For Coaches "How to Read the Diagrams" legend):

- pass:    DOTTED arrow — ball travels between players
- screen:  line ending in a PERPENDICULAR BAR — pick set by a teammate
- dribble: ZIG-ZAG / WAVY line with arrow — player moves WHILE dribbling

- handoff: TWO PARALLEL BARS perpendicular to the path — hand-off between players
