# Two-Line Standing Still Drill

## Objective
Teach all fundamental pass types (chest, bounce, overhead, one-handed, outlet, baseball) in a controlled stationary setting before progressing to passing on the move.

## Setup
- Players line up in two-player pairs about 15 feet (4.5 m) apart
- Each two-player pair has one ball
- Multiple pairs can work simultaneously across the full gym
- For advanced (baseball pass): spread pairs 30 feet (9 m) apart

## Execution

### Beginner
1. Players line up in pairs about 15 feet apart
2. Players throw chest passes to each other for 1 minute
3. Then bounce passes for 1 minute
4. Then overhead (flip) passes for 1 minute

### Intermediate
- Players throw one-handed chest passes to each other for 1 minute
- Then one-handed bounce passes for 1 minute

### Advanced
- Players throw overhead outlet passes to each other for 1 minute (spread to 30 feet apart)
- Then baseball passes for 1 minute (at 30-foot spacing)

### Options (All Skill Levels)
1. Players fake a pass before delivering a pass (e.g., fake a chest pass, then throw a bounce pass)
2. Players step around an invisible defender to make the pass: step left one time, step right the next

## Coaching Points
- **Chest Pass**: Pads not palms; thumbs up → thumbs down on release; step forward to pass; receiver steps to the ball watching it all the way in
- **Bounce Pass**: Same as chest pass but aim at a floor point so the ball reaches the receiver between beltline and chest
- **Overhead Pass**: Ball slightly overhead, elbows flexed, forearms perpendicular; wrist flip not arm throw; thumbs finish pointing toward receiver
- **One-Handed Chest**: One hand on side, one behind ball; push with the back hand; both arms fully extended on follow-through
- **Overhead Outlet**: Ball completely overhead, almost no elbow flex; violent arm forward motion; wrist flip to finish
- **Baseball Pass**: Sideways to receiver; non-throwing hand below ball for balance; no wrist twist — straight through release; step forward with opposite foot on release
- Enforce receiver stepping to the ball on every pass type

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Stationary chest → bounce → overhead (1 min each)
2. **Intermediate**: Add one-handed chest and one-handed bounce
3. **Advanced**: Add overhead outlet and baseball pass; spread to 30 feet
4. **All levels option**: Add pass fakes and stepping around invisible defenders
