# Triceps Pushdown — Cable Machine (Basketball Strength Training)

## Objective
Isolate and strengthen the triceps brachii to support explosive elbow extension required for chest passes, shot-blocking, boxing out, and pushing through contact in the post.

## Setup
- **Equipment**: Cable machine with short bar attachment (or rope, triangle handle, or towel)
- **Players**: 1 athlete
- **Location**: Weight room

## Execution
1. **Beginning Position**: Attach a short bar to the cable machine. Stand facing the machine, grabbing each end of the bar with one hand. Keep arms close to the sides of the body and flex the torso forward slightly at the **same angle as the cable**.
2. Keeping **upper arms next to the torso**, extend the elbows and push through the wrists until elbows are **fully extended**.
3. Allow elbows to flex back up to at least **90 degrees** — move through the greatest range of motion possible **without allowing the elbows to leave the side of the body**.
4. Repeat for desired reps.

## Coaching Points
- Elbows should remain by the **side of the body throughout** — do not let them flare out or drift forward
- Keep the **core tight** to prevent forward flexion or the use of body momentum
- **Maintain an athletic stance** — do not hunch over the machine
- Do not use torso to jerk the weight; controlled elbow extension only

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Rope attachment — allows hands to pronate/supinate naturally; lighter load
2. **Intermediate**: Short bar or triangle handle; full range of motion through 90°+
3. **Advanced**: Single-arm with single-grip rope or handle (reveals bilateral strength asymmetries); lean against a wall with cable straight up and down for strict form

## Variations
- **Rope attachment**: Natural hand rotation, increased range of motion
- **Triangle handle**: Neutral grip option
- **Hand towel**: Drape around the bar — thicker grip challenge
- **Wall-lean variation**: Back against wall, cable straight up — eliminates all momentum
- **Single-arm**: One arm at a time with a single-grip rope or handle
