# Fill Shots Off Screen Drill Series

## Objective
Develop consistent catch-and-shoot technique on wing fill shots (15–20 feet) after setting up and using a screen.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter + passer (+ stationary screener or cone)
- **Court area**: Wing, both sides of the floor
- **Equipment**: Stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Screener position**: Near the wing extended area
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start**: Underneath the basket

## Execution
1. Shooter starts underneath the basket and approaches the stationary screener.
2. Shooter uses the screen and cuts to the **wing** to receive the pass.
3. Shooter catches in rhythm and takes a **fill shot** (catch-and-shoot) at **15–20 feet** from the basket.
4. Shooter holds the follow-through until the ball hits the floor.
5. After completing each set of fill shots, shoot **5 free throws** before the next set.
6. In the 100-rep version, alternate left and right sides of the floor.

## Coaching Points
- **"Get your feet moving toward the shot"** — arrive at the spot in motion, not flat-footed
- **"Inside foot first"** — inside pivot foot (nearer the basket) plants first to square shoulders
- Watch for ball dipping on the catch — "catch and rise in one motion"
- Passer must hit the shooting pocket — the shooter should not have to reach, step, or adjust
- "Freeze your follow-through" — hold until the ball hits the floor

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-7)**: Single side, 5 fills + 5 FTs × 5 sets. [S3, p.148]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-8)**: Single side, larger set. Target: 15/25 fills (60%), 21/25 FTs (84%). [S3, p.149]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-9)**: Both sides, 5 fills + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each side; or 10 fills + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets. Total 150 shots. Target: 60/100 fills (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.150-154]
