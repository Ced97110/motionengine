# 1-on-0 in the Box (Workout #7-1)

## Objective
Build ball-handling confidence and control under simulated pressure by working four key dribble moves within a confined space at the top of the key. [S3, p.172]

## Setup
- 1 player
- Top of the key — imaginary box (approximately free-throw line width, 2–3 ft deep)
- No equipment needed
- Coach sets the time interval

## Execution
1. Player positions himself at the top of the key inside an imaginary box.
2. Begins dribbling and works through four dribble moves **within the box** for the full time interval:
   - **Strong dribble**: pound the ball hard, stay low, protect the ball
   - **Rocker dribble**: dribble forward and then rock back, simulating a hesitation move
   - **Rocker-to-crossover dribble**: complete the rocker move, then cross the ball in front to change hands
   - **Switch dribble**: smooth hand-to-hand transfer while maintaining low athletic stance
3. Player must stay inside the box throughout — stepping out of the box defeats the purpose.
4. Progress through time intervals:
   - 15 seconds
   - 30 seconds
   - 45 seconds
   - 60 seconds

## Coaching Points
- **Stay low**: dribbling low reduces the chance of a deflection or steal — hip crease below 45°.
- **Pound the ball**: a strong dribble snaps back quickly, giving the player more control and making it harder for defenders to pick off.
- **Eyes up**: even in a 1-on-0 drill, the player should habituate looking up, not at the ball.
- **Stay in the box**: the confinement forces the player to use change-of-direction moves efficiently rather than relying on space.
- Coaching cue: *"Low hips, hard pound, eyes up — stay home."*

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: 15-second intervals only; use two moves (strong dribble + crossover) before adding the rocker.
2. **Intermediate**: 30-45 second intervals; all four moves on command from coach.
3. **Advanced**: 60-second intervals; add a live defender (see [[drill-1-on-1-box-dribbling]]).

- [[creating-your-own-shot]] — ball-handling confidence is the foundation of shot creation off the dribble
