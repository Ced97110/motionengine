# Combo Sidestep/Forward-Back (Bag Drill)

## Objective
Improve the ability to change direction, flexibility, high-knee action, and quickness of foot movements through a 7-bag combination pattern. [S8, p.116]

## Setup
- 1 player
- 7 agility bags arranged in an offset pattern (see diagram description below)
- Open court or grass area with approximately 10 yards of space

<!-- DIAGRAM: 7 bags arranged in offset formation — bags 1-2 side by side, bags 3 and 6 positioned 5 yards forward from the lateral sections, bags 4-5 lateral, bag 7 at far end. See [S8, p.116] for original diagram. -->

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance with hands and arms away from the body.
2. **Sprint laterally** over bags 1 and 2 (sidestep across).
3. **Sprint 5 yards (4.6 meters)** to the front of bag 3, then shuffle laterally past it.
4. **Backpedal 5 yards (4.6 meters)** and then laterally step over bags 4 and 5.
5. **Sprint 5 yards (4.6 meters)** to the front of bag 6, then shuffle laterally past it.
6. **Backpedal 5 yards (4.6 meters)** and laterally step over bag 7. [S8, p.116]

## Coaching Points
- Arms should be away from the body to help with balance and momentum
- Stay in a two-point stance — do not stand up between direction changes
- Transition between movements must be explosive — "no dead steps between gears"
- Keep shoulders square throughout the entire sequence
- The shuffle sections: do not cross feet

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through the sequence to memorize direction changes, then add speed
2. **Intermediate**: Full-speed execution, focus on seamless transitions
3. **Advanced**: Add a catch-and-shoot or defensive read at the conclusion of the drill
