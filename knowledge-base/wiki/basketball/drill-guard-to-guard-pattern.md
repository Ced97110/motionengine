# Guard-to-Guard Pass Pattern (LA Offense)

## Objective
Teach the guard-to-guard entry option of the LA Offense, featuring a guard position exchange, backdoor-threatening inside cuts, back picks, and simultaneous weak-side and strong-side scoring actions.

## Setup
- Half court, 5 players
- Two-guard front: O1 and O2 at guard positions
- O3 at strong-side wing; O4 at weak-side forward; O5 at high post
- Either guard can initiate to either side

## Execution
1. O1 passes to O2 (guard-to-guard entry) and sets an inside screen for O3.
2. O3 exchanges position with O1 and receives a direct pass from O2.
3. O3 passes to O1.
4. Using a jab step, O3 tries to get an inside ball cut while diving to the strong-side block.
5. O2 dives to the weak-side block after seeing O3 pass to O1.
6. O5 watches both O3 and O2 clear the post area, then pops out to receive the pass from O1.
7. O5 receives the pass, turns, faces the defender, and always protects the ball.
8. As O1 passes to O5, O3 steps up and sets a back pick on O1's defender. An inside cut is preferred because X3 must help if X1 is screened.
9. If O1 is open off the back screen, O1 is O5's first pass option.
10. After setting the back screen, O3 takes two steps and opens to the ball looking for a catch-and-shoot jump shot.
11. On the weak side, O4 pins down on O2, who comes off looking for a catch-and-shoot or a curl to the basket.
12. If O2 pops and catches, O2 also has a two-man game option with O4 — O4 can post up or run a sideline PnR.
13. O5 keeps the dribble available as a dribble-handoff escape if needed.

## Coaching Points
- O3 and O2's dives to the blocks must be simultaneous and aggressive to clear the post area for O5.
- O5's pop-out must be timed to when both O3 and O2 have cleared — "wait until the lane is empty, then come."
- O3's back screen on X1 must be legal — set with a wide base, no moving screen.
- "See the court" cue for O5: read all three options (O1 layup, O3 catch-and-shoot, O2 weak-side) before deciding.
- O4's pin-down on O2 is the weak-side scoring action — emphasize read between pop (catch-and-shoot) and curl (layup).

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through the exchange, dive, and pop sequence with no defense.
2. **Intermediate**: Add token defense; practice O5's three-read sequence.
3. **Advanced**: Live 5-on-5 with the guard-to-guard trigger; allow all sub-options including O2/O4 PnR.
