# Barrel Roll to Reaction (Whole-Body Reaction)

## Objective
Improve mental processing speed, total-body reaction time to visual and audible stimuli, and total-body agility. [S8, p.215]

## Setup
- 1 player, open floor space
- No equipment required
- Coach/partner provides audible or visual cues

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance (athletic ready position) with the **left foot forward**.
2. Bend forward and begin to fall forward under control.
3. Just before making contact with the floor, **roll over the left shoulder** (barrel roll).
4. Complete the roll and return immediately to a ready position.
5. React instantly to the coach's audible or visual cue — execute the commanded action (sprint, shuffle, jump, etc.) as fast as possible.

## Coaching Points
- Roll over the shoulder, **not the neck** — protect the cervical spine
- The return to ready position should be explosive — do not pause after the roll
- Eyes up immediately upon standing to receive the coach's cue
- "Roll smooth, pop up fast, eyes up — go!"

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Practice the forward roll alone without the reaction cue
2. **Intermediate**: Add simple reaction commands (direction of sprint)
3. **Advanced (Complex Variation)**: Add a sport-specific skill after the roll — defensive slide, vertical jump, shot simulation, or other sport-specific action [S8, p.215]
