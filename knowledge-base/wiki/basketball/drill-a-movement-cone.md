# A-Movement Cone Drill

## Objective
Improve multi-directional agility by combining sprint, bilateral lateral shuffle, and backpedal in a compact A-shaped cone pattern.

## Setup
- 1 player
- 5 cones in an A-shape:
  - Cone 1: bottom left (starting point)
  - Cone 5: bottom right (10 yards from cone 1)
  - Cone 2: 5 yards in front of cone 1
  - Cone 3: 5 yards in front of cone 5
  - Cone 4: 5 yards in front of, and between, cones 2 and 3 (apex of the A)

<!-- DIAGRAM: Bottom row — cones 1 and 5, 10 yards apart. Middle row — cones 2 and 3, 5 yards ahead of 1 and 5. Apex — cone 4, 5 yards ahead of and between 2 and 3. See [S8, p.105] -->

## Execution
1. Start at cone 1.
2. **Sprint** diagonally from cone 1 to cone 2.
3. **Shuffle** laterally from cone 2 to cone 3.
4. **Shuffle back** from cone 3 to cone 2.
5. **Sprint** diagonally from cone 2 to cone 4 (apex).
6. **Backpedal** from cone 4 to cone 5.

## Coaching Points
- Shuffle segments: no crossing of feet; stay in defensive stance
- Sprint diagonally with forward lean and explosive first step
- Backpedal from cone 4 must be controlled — lean forward, short quick steps
- "Sprint, shuffle, sprint, backpedal" — call out each phase to reinforce pattern memory

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Reduce distances by half; learn the A-pattern before adding speed.
2. **Intermediate**: Full distances at game speed.
3. **Advanced**: Time each rep; compete; reverse the direction (start at cone 5, finish at cone 1).

- [[defensive-transition-principles]] — shuffle segments reinforce defensive positioning habits
