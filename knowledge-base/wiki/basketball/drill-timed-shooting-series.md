# Timed Shooting Drill Series

## Objective
Build shooting volume and accuracy from three court zones (inside, outside, three-point) under game-like time pressure, while simultaneously training outlet passing and offensive rebounding.

## Setup
- **Players**: 2 (Passer/Rebounder P, Shooter S)
- **Court area**: Half court — passer restricted to inside the free throw lane; shooter restricted to the designated zone
- **Equipment**: 1 basketball, shot clock or stopwatch
- **Zones**: Inside lane, outside lane (midrange), behind three-point line

## Execution
1. Passer (P) stands inside the free throw lane with the ball.
2. Shooter (S) positions in the designated shooting zone (inside, outside, or 3PT).
3. P passes to S; S catches with feet and hands already ready and shoots as quickly as possible.
4. P sprints to rebound, makes an outlet pass back to S, and the sequence continues without stopping.
5. After the designated time limit (3 minutes per zone), P and S swap roles.
6. Record the number of shot attempts and made baskets for each shooter from each zone.
7. Require a specific move before shooting (especially for inside shots) — vary the required move each day.

## Zone Standards (Per Shooter, 3 Minutes)

### Inside Shots
- Expected attempts: **27–30**
- 70% accuracy = ~19–21 made
- 60% accuracy = ~16–18 made
- 50% accuracy = ~14–15 made

### Outside Shots (Midrange)
- Expected attempts: **24–27**
- 50% accuracy = ~12–14 made

### Three-Point Shots
- Expected attempts: **21–24**
- 50% accuracy = ~10–12 made

## Coaching Points
- Shooter must have **feet and hands ready** before catching — no catch-then-set-up
- Passer should deliver the ball quickly but accurately — outlet pass tempo drives the drill pace
- "Catch, set, shoot — no wasted movements"
- Vary the required move on inside shots daily: drop step, up-and-under, jump hook
- Record all makes and attempts; use them to set and track individual goals
- Coach observes shooting footwork, release, and follow-through on every rep

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Stationary catch-and-shoot, no required move
2. **Intermediate**: Required shot fake before every shot
3. **Advanced**: Alternate required moves (shot fake, jab step, rip step) — add a second defender for inside shots
