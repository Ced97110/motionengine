# Fade Shot Workout Series (Workouts #5-1, #5-2, #5-3)

## Objective
Develop the fade shot off a screen — catching the ball as a defender tries to go over the screen, and firing a 15–20 foot jump shot away from the basket.

## Setup
- **Players**: 2–3 (shooter, passer, optional rebounder/screener object)
- **Court area**: Under the basket to the wing, both sides
- **Equipment**: 1 basketball, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Screener position**: Wing area (stationary) [S3]

## Execution

### Fade Shot Mechanics
1. Shooter starts under the basket and makes a V-cut to set up the screen
2. Come off the screen on the fade — move away from the screen toward the sideline/corner
3. Receive the pass from the passer at the top of the key
4. Catch ready to shoot: set feet quickly, align elbow, fire
5. Target distance: 15–20 feet from the basket [S3]

### Workout #5-1: 25 Fade Shots
- 5 sets: Fade × 5 → FTs × 5
- Total: 25 fade shots + 25 free throws [S3]

### Workout #5-2: 50 Fade Shots
- Shoot from left side of floor (5 Fade → 5 FTs) × 5 = 25 fades from left
- Shoot from right side of floor (5 Fade → 5 FTs) × 5 = 25 fades from right
- Total: 50 fade shots + 50 free throws
- **Targets**: Fade 15-25 (60%), FT 21-25 (84%) [S3]

### Workout #5-3: 100 Fade Shots
**Left Side of Floor**: 5 sets of (Fade × 5 → FTs × 5) = 25 fades + 25 FTs
**Right Side of Floor**: 5 sets of (Fade × 5 → FTs × 5) = 25 fades + 25 FTs

OR (advanced version):
**Left Side**: 5 sets of (Fade × 10 → FTs × 5) = 50 fades + 25 FTs
**Right Side**: 5 sets of (Fade × 10 → FTs × 5) = 50 fades + 25 FTs
- Total: 100 fade shots + 50 free throws
- **Targets**: Fade 30-50 (60%), FT 43-50 (86%) [S3]

## Coaching Points
- The cutter must **set the defender up** before fading — don't just drift; sell the curl first
- "Fade AWAY from the screen — if you feel the defender going over, you go under"
- Catch with feet already moving toward the shot direction — don't stop and then reset
- The fade creates a longer shot; ensure the shot arc is high enough to compensate for distance
- Track FGA and FGM every session — target 60% on fade shots, 85%+ on free throws [S3]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout #5-1)**: 25 total fades from one side, no defender, passer waits for shooter to get open
2. **Intermediate (Workout #5-2)**: 50 total fades from both sides — develop both-direction competency
3. **Advanced (Workout #5-3)**: 100 total fades with higher volume per set; can add a live defender reading the screen
