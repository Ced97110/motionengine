# Cone Shuffle Warm-Up Drill Series

## Objective
Warm up basketball-specific lateral shuffles, forward sprints, change-of-direction transitions, and competitive footwork — mirroring the multi-directional demands of a game.

## Setup
- **Drill 1 (Basic)**: 3 cones — one at near free-throw line, one at half-court, one at far free-throw line
- **Drill 2 (Wide)**: Two cones 3 ft apart at half-court; two cones 6 ft apart at far free-throw line
- **Drill 4 (Multiple)**: Four cones at each free-throw line and half-court
- **Players**: 1+ (partner version needs 2)
- **Court**: Full court
- **Equipment**: Cones (3–8 depending on variation)

## Execution

### Drill 1 — Single Cone Shuffle
1. Start at the baseline.
2. Sprint toward the near free-throw line cone.
3. Shuffle laterally around the cone (no crossing feet) in one direction.
4. Sprint toward the half-court cone.
5. Shuffle around in the **opposite** direction.
6. Sprint to the far free-throw line cone and finish in the original direction.

### Drill 2 — Wide Cone Shuffle
1. Same structure as Drill 1 but two cones at half-court (3 ft apart) and two at the far free-throw line (6 ft apart).
2. Shuffle the full width between the cones at each station.
3. **Variation A**: Figure-8 pattern through the cones.
4. **Variation B**: Coach stands at cones and points left or right — player reads and reacts before shuffling.

### Drill 3 — Partner Cone Drill
1. Two players start 10 feet apart.
2. Both perform Drill 1 together from baseline to half-court.
3. Past half-court: one player receives a ball pass and becomes the offensive player; the other becomes a live defender.
4. Play out the possession all the way to the basket.

### Drill 4 — Multiple Cone Shuffle
1. Place 4 cones across the free-throw line (spacing ~3 ft apart) and another 4 at half-court.
2. Sprint from baseline to first cone set.
3. Weave through the cones: go in **front** of cone 1, **behind** cone 4.
4. Sprint to the half-court cones and weave in the **opposite** direction.
5. **Variation**: Run diagonally after the first shuffle to reach the far set — forces both left and right shuffle directions.

## Coaching Points
- "Never warm up at 100 percent speed — controlled, purposeful movement."
- Keep arms active and pumping during shuffles as if sprinting.
- **Do not drop the head or eyes** when transitioning from shuffle to forward sprint — maintain court vision.
- Feet must not cross during lateral shuffles — this is non-negotiable footwork discipline.
- Coach should walk among athletes to check knee alignment and head position during each drill.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Drill 1 — three single cones, full-court pace at ~70%
2. **Intermediate**: Drill 2 with figure-8 pattern; Drill 4 diagonal variation
3. **Advanced**: Drill 2 with coach reaction signal; Drill 3 with live 1-on-1 possession
