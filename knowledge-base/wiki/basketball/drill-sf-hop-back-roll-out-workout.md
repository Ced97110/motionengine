# Shot Fake (SF) Hop Back / Roll-Out Workouts

## Objective
Develop the three-part combination move of shot fake → hop back (to create space) → roll-out dribble (to attack the recovering defender), building a two-level counter system off the same shot fake setup.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court, midrange/perimeter areas
- Shot chart for tracking (FGA/FGM, FTA/FTM)

## Execution

### Workout #3-16 (40 total shots — introductory)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out Dribble **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out Dribble **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out Dribble **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out Dribble **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5

**Targets:** 12-20 FG (60%), 17-20 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-17 (80 total shots)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Left** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Right** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Left** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Right** × 10 | FTs × 10

**Targets:** 24-40 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-18 (120 total shots)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Left** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Right** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Left** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back → Roll Out **Right** × 20 | FTs × 10

**Targets:** 48-80 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Per Rep Execution:
1. Triple-threat position.
2. Shot fake.
3. 1 or 2 attack dribbles toward basket.
4. Explosive backward two-foot hop.
5. Initiate roll-out dribble in predetermined direction (away from imagined defender's recovery path).
6. Drive or pull up for shot at new spot.
7. Freeze follow-through.

## Coaching Points
- "Hop back first, then read the defense — if they close, roll out."
- The roll-out dribble must be decisive — pick a direction and attack it.
- Keep the roll-out dribble low; high dribbles during direction changes invite strips.
- Know before you start each rep whether you will roll left or right on this rep.
- FTs after every set — practice pressure.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Drill the hop-back only (without roll-out) using [[drill-sf-hop-back-workout]] first.
2. **Intermediate**: Add the roll-out as a stationary second move — hop back, plant, then deliberate roll-out dribble.
3. **Advanced**: Full game-speed execution in Workout #3-18 with consecutive attack finish.
