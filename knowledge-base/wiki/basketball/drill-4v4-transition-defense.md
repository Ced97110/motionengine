# 4-on-4 Full-Court Transition Defense Drill

## Objective
Train defenders to stop the ball, match up by proximity, and recover from a numbers disadvantage when one defender is momentarily out of the play — simulating a realistic transition scenario.

## Setup
- Full court
- Two equal lines of four players each
- **Defensive players**: lined up on the foul line, facing the baseline
- **Offensive players**: lined up on the baseline, facing the opposite basket
- Coach positioned at the sideline with the ball

## Execution
1. Coach passes the ball to an offensive player.
2. The defensive player directly in front of that offensive player must run to **touch the foul line** and then sprint back in defensive transition.
3. The remaining three defenders immediately retreat to stop the 4-vs-3 fast break that is developing.
4. The three defenders must:
   - Get back in transition
   - Match up by proximity (not by individual assignment)
   - Stop the ball
   - Protect the goal
5. The trailing fourth defender (who touched the foul line) must sprint to get **level with the ball** and pick up the free offensive player — who is likely not his original opponent.
6. The possession is complete only when the offense scores or the defensive team recovers/rebounds the ball. [S1, p.173]

## Coaching Points
- The three recovering defenders must not stand still — "sprint back, pick up the first open man inside"
- The trailing fourth defender must communicate arrival: "I've got 4!" or by number
- No uncontested layups — commit a hard foul if necessary rather than give up an and-one
- Big man back takes the first inside threat regardless of assignment
- "Level with the ball" — teach defenders to get even with or below the ball before making contact

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through positioning only — no dribbling. Coach points to where each defender should be at each moment.
2. **Intermediate**: Live drill with offense limited to no more than 3 dribbles before shooting — speeds up decision-making for defense.
3. **Advanced**: Full-speed live play; immediate role reversal if offense scores (upgrade to 5v5 transition).
