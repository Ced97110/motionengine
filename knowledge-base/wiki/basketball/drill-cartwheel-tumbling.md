# Cartwheel (Tumbling Drill)

## Objective
Develop total-body agility and kinesthetic awareness through a lateral cartwheel movement pattern. [S8, p.125]

## Setup
- 1 player
- Soft grass or open court area
- No additional equipment needed

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance.
2. Flex laterally to the left and put your left hand on the ground.
3. Continue to turn, putting your right hand on the other side of the left hand.
4. As your feet go over you, the right foot lands first and then the left foot lands on the other side.
5. Perform the drill to **both sides**. [S8, p.125]

## Coaching Points
- Arms must be fully extended during the cartwheel — bent elbows collapse the movement
- Keep the body straight through the inverted phase — a tight core prevents collapse
- Land one foot at a time in a balanced stagger; do not jump-land with both feet simultaneously
- Practice to both sides — the non-dominant side will be weaker and requires extra reps
- "Straight line" is the coaching cue — all hand and foot placements should be on a single line

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Assisted cartwheel with a partner holding the hips through the rotation
2. **Intermediate**: Full cartwheel as described, both sides
3. **Advanced (Complex Variations)**:
   - Add a sprint in any direction before or after
   - React to a visual or auditory cue after the cartwheel
   - Add a sport-specific skill after completing the movement [S8, p.125]
