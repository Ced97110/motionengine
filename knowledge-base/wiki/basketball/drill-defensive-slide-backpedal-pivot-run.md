# Defensive Slide, Backpedal, Pivot, and Run

## Objective
Build the complete defensive footwork pattern combining lateral sliding, backpedaling, pivoting, and forward running in one continuous movement, transitioning from individual pattern work to a live 1-on-1 closeout drill. [S1, p.58]

## Setup
- Half court (paint area and wing)
- 1 defensive player (solo or with a mirror partner)
- Optional: a coach with a loose ball for the live 1-on-1 progression

## Execution

### Phase 1 — Solo Pattern (Figure 5.14)
1. Defensive player faces the baseline at one end of the lane in defensive stance.
2. **Lateral slide** across the court to the sideline (staying low, knees bent).
3. **Lateral slide back** to the low block.
4. **Backpedal** up to the foul line.
5. **Pivot** at the foul line.
6. **Run** to the foul line extended (wing area).
7. Repeat for multiple reps.

### Phase 2 — Mirror Drill with Partner (Figure 5.15)
1. Add an offensive player facing the baseline and facing the defender.
2. Both players mirror each other through the same pattern: slide, backpedal, pivot, run to the wing.
3. Offensive player mirrors; defensive player reacts and maintains defensive positioning throughout.

### Phase 3 — Live 1-on-1 (Figure 5.16)
1. After the mirror movement, coach drops a loose ball for the offensive player to pick up.
2. Two players play live 1-on-1 from the wing. [S1, p.58]

## Coaching Points
- Stay low throughout every slide — "maintain a low center of gravity"
- Backpedal must be controlled — don't fall backward; maintain balance and vision
- The pivot at the foul line must be crisp and explosive — not gradual
- In the mirror phase, the defensive player must REACT to the offensive player's movement, not anticipate it
- In Phase 3, the defender is already in position and mentally prepared before the live ball hits the floor

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Solo pattern (Phase 1) — learn the footwork sequence
2. **Intermediate**: Mirror drill (Phase 2) — react to a live partner
3. **Advanced**: Live 1-on-1 after mirror movement (Phase 3) — full game application
