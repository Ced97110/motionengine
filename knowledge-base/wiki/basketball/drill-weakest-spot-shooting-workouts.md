# Weakest Spot Shooting Workouts

## Objective
Force players to confront and improve their lowest-percentage shooting locations through a make-based quota system — the drill only ends when the shooter reaches the target makes from each of their three weakest spots.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court
- Player pre-selects **3 spots** on the floor where they have the most **trouble** making shots
- Self-pass: throw ball to self, sprint to ball, square up, shoot
- Sprint after every rebound — do not walk

## Execution
1. Before beginning, the player identifies and commits to 3 specific spots where they struggle most.
2. Starting at Spot 1, the player throws the ball out, sprints to it, squares up, and shoots.
3. The player chases every rebound at full speed.
4. The player shoots from Spot 1 until they reach the make quota (10, 20, or 30 — see variants).
5. Attempts do not matter — only makes count toward the quota.
6. Once quota is met at Spot 1, move to Spot 2. Then Spot 3.

## Workout Variants

### Workout #11-13: 10 Weakest Spot Shots
**Make target:** 10 per spot | **Total makes:** 30 [S3, pp.217-218]

| Spot | Goal |
|------|------|
| Spot 1 (weakest) | Make 10 |
| Spot 2 (weakest) | Make 10 |
| Spot 3 (weakest) | Make 10 |

### Workout #11-14: 20 Weakest Spot Shots
**Make target:** 20 per spot | **Total makes:** 60 [S3, pp.218-219]

| Spot | Goal |
|------|------|
| Spot 1 (weakest) | Make 20 |
| Spot 2 (weakest) | Make 20 |
| Spot 3 (weakest) | Make 20 |

### Workout #11-15: 30 Weakest Spot Shots
**Make target:** 30 per spot | **Total makes:** 90 [S3, pp.219-220]

| Spot | Goal |
|------|------|
| Spot 1 (weakest) | Make 30 |
| Spot 2 (weakest) | Make 30 |
| Spot 3 (weakest) | Make 30 |

## Coaching Points
- **This is a development drill, not a confidence drill.** The player will miss more and must stay mentally tough. Emphasize mechanics over results on misses.
- **Coach should verify spot selection** — ensure spots are real game locations where the player genuinely struggles, not arbitrary.
- **Pair with favorite spots workouts** — run [[drill-favorite-spot-shooting-workouts]] first to build confidence, then move to weakest spot workouts in the same session.
- **Make-based accountability** — the player cannot move on until the quota is hit. This teaches persistence and mental resilience.
- **Sprint every rep** — do not walk after rebounds.
- Track FGA, FGM, and % per spot per date to show improvement over time.

## Common Mistakes
1. Choosing spots that are merely uncomfortable, not truly weak → correction: use [[shot-charting]] data to identify authentic weak spots
2. Losing proper mechanics under frustration → correction: coach calls out cues: "elbow in, follow through, freeze"
3. Walking to rebounds → correction: sprint every rep
4. Quitting the spot before reaching quota → correction: the quota is non-negotiable — this is the point of the drill

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Start with [[drill-favorite-spot-shooting-workouts]] — build confidence first
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #11-13 (10 makes per weak spot)
3. **Advanced**: Workout #11-14 or #11-15 (20–30 makes per weak spot)
