# Cooldown — Shoulder and Wrist Circuit

## Objective
Restore range of motion in the shooting shoulder and wrist after a session of catches, releases, and contested shots.

## Setup
- 1 player (works alone or in a team line)
- Any open space, sideline or baseline
- No equipment required; a basketball is optional for the wrist segment

## Execution
1. **Slow shoulder circles — 30 seconds each direction.** Stand tall, arms relaxed at your sides. Roll your shoulders forward in big, slow circles for 30 seconds, then reverse. Cue: "Drag the shoulder blades down your back at the bottom of every circle."
2. **Cross-body shoulder hold — 30 seconds per side.** Pull your right arm across your chest with your left hand, just above the elbow. Hold without bouncing. Switch arms. Cue: "Breathe into the back of the shoulder you are stretching."
3. **Overhead reach with elbow pull — 30 seconds per side.** Raise your right elbow up next to your ear, drop the right hand behind your neck, and gently pull the right elbow with your left hand. Switch sides. Cue: "Tall through the ribs — do not arch your low back to fake the reach."
4. **Wrist rolls — 30 seconds.** Hands clasped in front of you, roll the wrists in slow figure-eights. Reverse direction halfway through. Cue: "Loose, not forced — small joints get angry when you crank them."
5. **Shooting-hand finishes — 10 reps.** Lift your shooting hand in front of your face and snap a slow follow-through. Hold the finish for two counts each rep. Cue: "Long fingers, fingertips toward the rim, wrist relaxed at the top."

## Coaching Points
- This is recovery work, not a workout. Slow tempo wins.
- Players should feel a stretch, never a pinch. Pinching means back off the angle.
- Pair with quiet talk — coaches use this window to debrief individual shooters.
- "Breathe through your nose, out through your mouth, every rep."

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Hold each segment for 20 seconds.
2. **Advanced**: Add a five-second isometric squeeze of the shoulder blades together at the top of the shoulder rolls.
