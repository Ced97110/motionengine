# Dribble Fade Shot Drill Series

## Objective
Train the shooter to read a high-angle closeout off a screen and attack the baseline with a live-ball dribble fade to create any scoring opportunity.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter + passer (+ optional live defender for advanced version)
- **Court area**: Wing, both sides of the floor
- **Equipment**: Stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Screener position**: Near the wing, creating a screen the shooter sets up from under the basket
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start**: Underneath the basket

## Execution
1. Shooter starts underneath the basket and approaches the stationary screener to set up the screen.
2. Shooter uses the screen and cuts to the wing to receive a pass from the passer at the top of the key.
3. Shooter identifies that the defender closes out on a **high angle** (leaving the baseline open).
4. Shooter establishes a **dominant pivot foot** immediately on the catch.
5. Shooter attacks the baseline with a **dribble fade** — one or two purposeful dribbles toward the baseline.
6. Shooter uses **any available scoring opportunity** once the baseline has been attacked: pull-up jumper, floater, layup, reverse layup, or draw the foul.
7. After completing a set of dribble fades, shoot **5 free throws** before switching sides or moving to the next set.
8. Alternate left and right sides of the floor in the 100-rep version.

## Coaching Points
- **"Establish your pivot foot before you attack"** — the pivot foot must be set before the dribble
- Watch for players going baseline when it's not open — enforce the read first, attack second
- "Attack with purpose" — every dribble should have a destination, not just movement
- Free throws between sets reinforce free-throw routine and simulate in-game foul situations
- Hold follow-through on any pull-up or mid-range finish

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-5)**: Single side, 5 dribble fades + 5 FTs × 5 sets. Target: 15/25 fades (60%), 21/25 FTs (84%). [S3, p.141]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — part of #5-6)**: Both sides, 5 dribble fades + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each side. Target: 30/50 (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.142]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-6)**: Both sides, 10 dribble fades + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each side. Total 150 shots. Target: 60/100 (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.142-143]
