# Curl Shot Off a Screen Drill

## Objective
Develop a consistent catch-and-shoot curl shot at 10–15 feet in the elbow area, simulating the game situation where a cutter reads that the defender is trailing and curls tight around the screen toward the basket.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter, passer, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Court area**: Half court — elbow area (10–15 feet from basket)
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Screener position**: Wing or near elbow
- **Shot distance**: 10–15 feet (elbow area)

## Execution
1. Shooter begins underneath the basket and sets up the screen with a V-cut
2. Shooter curls tightly around the stationary screen, working toward the elbow area
3. Passer delivers the ball as the shooter arrives at the elbow catch point
4. Shooter catches at 10–15 feet and immediately shoots a curl shot (catch-and-shoot)
5. Maintain proper footwork — inside pivot foot, heel-to-toe step, balance, follow-through
6. After each set of 5 curl shots, shooter takes 5 free throws, then returns

## Coaching Points
- The curl read occurs when the defender is trailing directly behind the cutter — go tight around the screen toward the basket
- "Curl tight, don't float" — the closer to the screener's body you curl, the more separation you create
- 10–15 feet is close to the basket — this shot requires a compact, quick release
- Pre-set shooting hand during the cut so the ball goes up immediately on the catch
- Freeze the follow-through until the ball hits the floor
- Curl shots in the elbow area have a high percentage — treat them like layups, not contested threes

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-13)**: 5 curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets. One side of floor. [S3, p.152]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-14)**: 15–25 curls made (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). One side. [S3, p.153]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-15)**: Split left/right — 5 curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets per side. Targets: 30–50 curls (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.153-155]
4. **Elite (150 shots — Workout #5-15 extended)**: 10 curls + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 60–100 curls (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.155]
