# Curl Shots Off Screen Drill Series

## Objective
Build consistent catch-and-shoot shooting from the elbow area (10–15 ft) off a curl cut when the defender is chasing over the top of the screen.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter + passer (+ stationary screener or cone)
- **Court area**: Elbow and mid-post area, both sides of the floor
- **Equipment**: Stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Screener position**: Near the three-second lane, creating a screen the shooter sets up from underneath
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start**: Underneath the basket

## Execution
1. Shooter starts underneath the basket and approaches the stationary screener.
2. Shooter curls **tightly around the screen** — shoulder-to-shoulder contact with the screener — to the **elbow area** (10–15 feet from the basket).
3. Shooter catches the pass from the top of the key in rhythm at the elbow.
4. Shooter squares to the basket on the catch and takes a **curl shot** from 10–15 feet.
5. Shooter holds the follow-through until the ball hits the floor.
6. Shoot **5 free throws** after each set of curl shots.
7. In the 100-rep version, alternate left and right sides of the floor.

## Coaching Points
- **"Rub shoulders with the screener"** — a tight curl = separation; a wide curl = no advantage
- Shooter's **destination is the elbow**, not the wing and not under the basket
- **"Square on the catch"** — pivot and face the basket in one motion as the ball arrives
- Passer timing is critical — ball must arrive as the cutter arrives at the elbow
- "Hold the follow-through" until the ball hits the floor
- Watch for shooters drifting past the elbow — elbow is the target, not deeper

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-13)**: Single side, 5 curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets. [S3, p.162]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-14)**: Single side. Target: 15/25 curls (60%), 21/25 FTs (84%). [S3, p.163]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-15)**: Both sides, 5 curls + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each; or 10 curls + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets. Total 150 shots. Target: 60/100 curls (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.164-170]
