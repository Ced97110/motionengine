# Shot Fake (SF) Big Hop Workouts

## Objective
Develop an explosive two-foot gather (big hop) pull-up jump shot off a shot fake in all four direction/dribble combinations, building a finishing weapon for tight spaces and shot-blocker environments.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court, midrange to near-paint areas
- Shot chart for tracking

## Execution

### Workout #3-10 (80 total shots)
- SF 1-Dribble Big Hop **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 1-Dribble Big Hop **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- (Repeat 1-dribble L/R two more times)
- SF 2-Dribble Big Hop **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Big Hop **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- (Repeat 2-dribble L/R two more times)

**Targets:** 24-40 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-11 (120 total shots)
- Same structure with 10 reps per set, 5 FTs per interval

**Targets:** 48-80 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (80%) [S3]

### Workout #3-12 (240 total shots)
- Same structure with 20 reps per set, 10 FTs per interval

**Targets:** 97-160 FG (60%), 68-80 FT (85%) [S3]

### Per Rep Execution:
1. Triple-threat starting position.
2. Execute convincing shot fake.
3. Attack with 1 or 2 hard dribbles in designated direction.
4. On final dribble, gather the ball and BOTH FEET leave and land simultaneously — wide stance, bent knees — the "big hop."
5. Land balanced, immediately load and explode upward into pull-up jump shot.
6. Freeze follow-through.

## Coaching Points
- "Land with both feet at the same time — like you're jumping into a puddle."
- The big hop is a SIMULTANEOUS two-foot landing; if one foot lands before the other, you've reverted to a stride stop.
- Stay low through the attack dribble going into the hop — don't stand up early.
- The hop should be forward (toward the basket), not sideways.
- Free throws after every set — simulate game composure.
- Use the shot chart every workout; target 60% FG at game pace.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Practice the big hop gather without a dribble — jump into the two-foot landing, shoot. Get comfortable with the simultaneous landing.
2. **Intermediate**: Add 1-dribble attack, use Workout #3-10 volume (5 reps).
3. **Advanced**: Full Workout #3-12 (20 reps × 8 sets) at game speed.
