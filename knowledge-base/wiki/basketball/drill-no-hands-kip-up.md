# No-Hands Kip-Up (Master Step of Kip-Up Chain)

## Objective
Execute the kip-up with arms NEVER touching the floor, powered entirely by waist/hip and leg drive — the pinnacle expression of explosive total-body agility from the ground.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Flat open floor or mat
- No equipment needed
- Prerequisite: master [[drill-wushu-kip-up]] (Step 9)
- **Avoid if neck problems are present** [S9, p.14]

## Execution
1. Sit on the floor with knees bent and heels on the ground.
2. Roll back and swing legs up — **keep arms to sides at all times; they must NEVER touch the floor**.
3. Roll onto upper-back and shoulders. Squeeze into a tight ball with bent knees close to the head.
4. **"Unravel"** — explode legs in an arc UP then DOWN; keep arms off the floor and to the side.
5. As feet touch down, keep torso and hands forward.
6. **Land on balls of both feet** into a squatting position. Keep arms to sides as you stand upright.
7. Squat down, roll back, and repeat.

## Coaching Points
- Power is generated entirely by **waist and legs** — the neck only acts as a lever momentarily, if at all [S9, p.14]
- "If there is a more impressive — or explosive — way to power up off the floor, then humans haven't invented it yet!" [S9, p.14]
- Do not attempt this with neck problems
- Arms staying to the sides (not crossing the chest yet) is the standard version; crossing arms over chest is the hardest variation
- **Elite progressions:**
  - Begin from lying flat on the floor (no rolling momentum)
  - Straight-leg style (as in Step 8)
  - Arms crossed over chest (most brutal)

## Progressions
1. **Intermediate prerequisite**: [[drill-wushu-kip-up]] with arms at right-angles
2. **Advanced**: Standard no-hands kip-up from seated roll
3. **Elite**: Start from lying flat on floor; OR straight-leg style; OR arms crossed over chest [S9, p.14]
