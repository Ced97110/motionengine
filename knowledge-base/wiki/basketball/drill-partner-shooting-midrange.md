# Partner Shooting (Midrange)

## Objective
Develop midrange catch-and-shoot mechanics with a live passer while simultaneously reinforcing quality passing habits for the feeder.

## Setup
- 2 players per basket (can scale to entire team with multiple baskets)
- Half court, 7 spots around the basket at midrange distance
- 1 basketball

## Execution
1. Shooter starts in a balanced athletic stance at the chosen spot.
2. Shooter **calls for the ball** (verbally, with eye contact, and with hand target shown).
3. Passer delivers the ball from **under the basket** to the shooter's shot pocket.
4. Shooter takes a one-two step to meet the ball, catches it in the shot pocket, sights the target, and shoots.
5. Shooter lands in a balanced position and **freezes the follow-through**.
6. Follow-up any miss with a **weak-handed layup** (passer gets the rebound and delivers the next pass).
7. Shoot from 7 spots around the basket — either a set number of makes per spot or strings style.
8. Players alternate as shooter and passer on a schedule (every set, or every make/miss depending on preferred rotation).

## Coaching Points
- **Shooter must call for the ball every rep.** No exceptions — if they don't call, the passer doesn't throw.
- **Passer must deliver a good pass.** "If you never practice throwing good passes in practice, you will never throw them in games." The passer's role is just as important as the shooter's.
- **Shooter strives to catch in a balanced position and land in a balanced position.** Both the catch and the landing should be evaluated.
- **Freeze the follow-through.** Many players have a tendency to drop both hands; the coach should watch and correct immediately.
- **No high-low-high motion.** If the shooter is catching high and dipping, the pass is arriving too high — the passer must hit the shot pocket.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Shoot 10 from each spot, focus on form only. Passer stands still under the basket.
2. **Intermediate**: Progress to making 10 from each spot. Add strings style competition.
3. **Advanced**: Add the **Closeout Shooting** progression — passer closes out after delivering the ball (see [[drill-closeout-shooting]]).
