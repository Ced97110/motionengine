# Shot Fake / Step Fake Full Workout Series (Workouts 3-1 to 3-5)

## Objective
Develop all scoring opportunities off the shot fake/step fake live-ball move — including stride stop, step-through, big hop, hop back, roll out, crossover dribble, and attack lay-ups — through a progressive series of structured workouts.

## Setup
- 1 player, half court
- Ball required; rebounder or ball rack helpful
- Catching off a live pass or spin-out to self both acceptable
- Free throws at the free-throw line between segments
- **All reps at game speed** [S3, p.13]

## Execution

### Workout 3-1: 130-Shot Comprehensive Shot Fake Workout
This workout covers every scoring option off the shot fake/step fake. Perform 5 reps of each segment, both left and right, with free throws between segments [S3, pp.13-14]:

1. **Spot Shots** — 5 reps (warm-up)
2. **FTs** — 5
3. **SF 1/2-Dribble Stride Stop (L)** — 5 reps: Shot fake, 1 or 2 dribbles, stride stop into a jump shot (left hand leads)
4. **SF 1/2-Dribble Stride Stop (R)** — 5 reps
5. **FTs** — 5
6. **SF 1/2-Dribble Stride Stop / Step-Through (L)** — 5 reps: After the stride stop, fake the shot and step through for a lay-up (left)
7. **SF 1/2-Dribble Stride Stop / Step-Through (R)** — 5 reps
8. **FTs** — 5
9. **SF 1/2-Dribble Big Hop (L)** — 5 reps: Explosive forward hop off 1-2 dribbles, power finish at the rim (left)
10. **SF 1/2-Dribble Big Hop (R)** — 5 reps
11. **FTs** — 5
12. **SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s (L)** — 5 reps: Shot fake, 1 dribble, hop back behind the arc for a three (left)
13. **SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s (R)** — 5 reps
14. **FTs** — 5
15. **SF 1/2-Dribble Hop Back (L)** — 5 reps: Shot fake, 1-2 dribbles, hop back into a mid-range pull-up (left)
16. **SF 1/2-Dribble Hop Back (R)** — 5 reps
17. **FTs** — 5
18. **SF 1/2-Dribble HB to Roll Out Dribble (L)** — 5 reps: Hop back then roll out with an outside dribble to a new angle (left)
19. **SF 1/2-Dribble HB to Roll Out Dribble (R)** — 5 reps
20. **FTs** — 5
21. **SF 1/2-Dribble HB to Crossover Dribble (L)** — 5 reps: Hop back, then crossover to attack the opposite direction (left)
22. **SF 1/2-Dribble HB to Crossover Dribble (R)** — 5 reps
23. **FTs** — 5
24. **Game Speed 3s** — 15 reps (3 sets of 5): Full-speed three-pointers with no prescribed move — player creates the shot
- **Totals: ~130 shots** (FG target: 60%; 3PT: 60%; FT: 85%) [S3, p.14]

---

### Workout 3-2: 260+ Shot Fake Workout
Double the reps from Workout 3-1 (10 per action, both sides) and increase game-speed 3s to 30 (6×5). All other structure identical. [S3, pp.14-16]
- **Totals: ~260 shots** (FG: 80-130 at 61%; 3PT: 30-50 at 60%; FT: 68-80 at 85%)

---

### Workout 3-3: 55 Shot Fake Attack Lay-Ups
Focuses exclusively on attacking the basket with lay-ups off the shot fake — the most direct scoring option [S3, p.16]:

1. **SF Attack Basket Lay-ups (L)** — 10 reps: Shot fake, attack straight to the basket, left-hand lay-up
2. **FTs** — 5
3. **SF Attack Basket Lay-ups (R)** — 10 reps
4. **FTs** — 5
5. **SF Attack/Crossover Lay-ups (L)** — 10 reps: Shot fake right, crossover drive left for a lay-up
6. **FTs** — 5
7. **SF Attack/Crossover Lay-ups (R)** — 10 reps: Shot fake left, crossover drive right
- **Totals: 55 shots** (FG: 38-40 at 95%; FT: 13-15 at 86%)

---

### Workout 3-4: 80 Shot Fake Stride Stop Shots
Specific focus on the stride stop pull-up off the shot fake — isolates this one scoring option at high volume [S3, pp.17-18]:

1. **SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop (L/R alternating)** — 5 reps each, ×2 rounds each side
2. **FTs** — 5 between each segment
3. **SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop (L/R alternating)** — 5 reps each, ×2 rounds each side
4. **FTs** — 5 between each segment
- **Totals: 80 shots** (FG: 24-40 at 60%; FT: 34-40 at 85%)

---

### Workout 3-5: 120 Shot Fake Stride Stop Shots
High-volume stride stop specialization — same structure as 3-4 but 10 reps per segment [S3, pp.19-20]:

1. **SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop (L/R alternating)** — 10 reps each, ×2 rounds each side
2. **FTs** — 5 between segments
3. **SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop (L/R alternating)** — 10 reps each, ×2 rounds each side
4. **FTs** — 5 between segments
- **Totals: 120 shots** (FG: 48-80 at 60%; FT: 34-40 at 80%)

## Coaching Points
- **The step fake is mandatory** — it physically establishes the pivot foot and eliminates the travel call; a shot fake alone is insufficient. [S3, p.12]
- **Two-foot jump stop on the catch** — all rep sequences begin with a two-foot catch to maximize pivot options. [S3, p.12]
- **Rip the ball to the opposite hip** — after every fake, the ball must come to the opposite hip to generate momentum in the attack direction. [S3, p.12]
- **Attack lay-ups must be finished** — do not just shoot — drive all the way to the rim and finish with the correct hand.
- **Game speed is non-negotiable** — slow reps build slow habits. Every sequence must be performed as if a live defender is present. [S3, p.13]
- **Use both hands** — every move is practiced left and right to build a two-way scorer.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout 3-3, 55 shots)**: Start with the attack lay-up drill — the most straightforward scoring option. Master the fake-and-drive before introducing more complex scoring reads.
2. **Intermediate (Workouts 3-1, 3-4, 3-5)**: Add the full range of scoring options from the catch point; build 1- and 2-dribble stride stops.
3. **Advanced (Workout 3-2, 260+ shots)**: High-volume comprehensive workout covering all options in a single session; add a live or active defender to force reads.
