# Fill Shot Off a Screen Drill

## Objective
Develop a consistent catch-and-shoot fill shot at 15–20 feet on the wing after using an off-ball screen, simulating the game situation where the defender plays the baseline.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter, passer, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Court area**: Half court — wing area
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Screener position**: Wing or elbow area
- **Shot distance**: 15–20 feet from the basket (wing, slightly inside the three-point line)

## Execution
1. Shooter begins underneath the basket
2. Shooter V-cuts to set up the stationary screener
3. Shooter uses the screen and fills to the wing, receiving the pass from the top of the key
4. Shooter catches the ball at 15–20 feet and immediately shoots a fill shot (catch-and-shoot)
5. Proper pivot foot, shot pocket, and follow-through must be maintained
6. After each set of 5 fill shots, shooter takes 5 free throws, then returns for next set

## Coaching Points
- Fill shots are catch-and-shoot opportunities — be ready to shoot before the ball arrives
- "Feet ready, hands ready" — pre-set shooting hand during the cut
- The fill read occurs when the defender closes out on the baseline or trails low, leaving the wing open
- Square up to the basket on the catch — use the inside pivot foot to land in a shooting stance
- Freeze the follow-through until the ball hits the floor
- 15–20 feet is midrange territory — focus on accuracy before adding distance

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-7)**: 5 fills + 5 FTs × 5 sets. One side of floor. [S3, p.144]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-8)**: 15–25 fills made (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). One side of floor. [S3, p.145]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-9)**: Split left/right side of floor — 5 fills + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 30–50 fills (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.145-146]
4. **Elite (150 shots — Workout #5-9 extended)**: 10 fills + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 60–100 fills (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.147]
