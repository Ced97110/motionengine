# Continuous 3-on-2 Fast-Break Drill with Trailer

## Objective
Develop fast-break defense (tandem), defensive rebounding, boxing out, transition offense, and physical conditioning in a competitive team drill. [S1, pp.208-210]

## Setup
- Divide the squad into two teams wearing different colored jerseys
- Teams line up on either side of mid-court, numbered in sequence
- Two defenders (X1, X2) start at one basket in a tandem defense
- Three offensive players (O1, O2, O3) line up behind half-court ready to attack
- O4 and O5 set up in a tandem defense at the far basket
- First team to score **11 baskets** wins
- A coach works with each team; penalties for flagrant fouls only

## Execution
1. **Attack phase:** O1, O2, O3 attack X1 and X2 in a 3-on-2 from behind half-court. X1 and X2 defend in a tandem (one up top stopping the ball, one protecting the basket).
2. **Trailer activation:** Once the ball crosses half-court, X3 runs onto the court from the sideline, touches the center circle, and sprints to help X1 and X2 — converting the defense from 2-on-3 to 3-on-3. This teaches defenders to stall until the trailer arrives.
3. **Scoring/rebound:** If O's score or if X's get a stop (steal or defensive rebound), all three X players immediately break toward the opposite basket.
4. **Reversal:** O4 and O5 are in a tandem at the far basket. They run up to meet the advancing X's at the mid-court line. Once the ball crosses half-court, O6 touches mid-court and sprints to help O4 and O5.
5. **Player rotation:** The offensive player who threw the **last pass** on the break goes to the end of his team's line at mid-court. The other two offensive players stay on court and become the tandem defenders.
6. Teams earn **1 point** for each score or defensive stop via rebound. Continue until one team reaches 11.

## Coaching Points
- Defensive emphasis: **stop the ball**, box out, defend the goal, don't let the ball handler freely shoot
- Tandem top defender: "Take the ball, force a pass — make them earn every foot."
- Tandem bottom defender: "Protect the rim, read the ball handler, don't commit early."
- Trailer: sprint to touch mid-court circle then close ground — communicate your arrival to your teammates
- Offensive player on the break: "Don't be the one who throws the last pass" — it costs you playing time

## Progressions
1. **Beginner:** Run as a 3-on-2 only (no trailer), emphasize tandem principles before adding complexity.
2. **Intermediate:** Add the trailer as described — defense learns to stall and buy time.
3. **Advanced:** Add a scoring incentive: team that holds the opponent under 5 in a row gets an automatic water break.
