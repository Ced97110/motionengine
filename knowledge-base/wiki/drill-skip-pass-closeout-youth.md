# Skip Pass and Closeout Drill (#150)

## Objective
Train help-side defenders to hold their interception stance against line-drive passes while closing out explosively on the lob skip pass to the weak-side receiver.

## Setup
- Half court
- 3 players: player 1 (passer at right wing/top), player 3 (weak-side wing), X3 (defender in help position)
- Rotation: 1 → X3 → 3 → end of line

<!-- DIAGRAM: Player 1 at right wing with ball. Player 3 on left wing/sideline. X3 in help position between them — two-thirds distance from player 1, one-third distance from player 3, one step off the line between 1 and 3. -->

## Execution
1. Player 1 begins by throwing a **line-drive pass** (even underhanded) to player 3. **X3 must be in interception stance and pick this pass off.** X3 passes back to player 1. Player 1 may repeat this as many times as desired to keep X3 honest. [S15, p.312]
2. Finally, player 1 **overhead lobs** a skip pass to player 3. X3 must:
   - Sprint straight toward player 3
   - Come under control with **slide steps** the last few steps
   - **Slide-step up the straddle** of the receiver, overplaying on the **free-foot side** — forcing a crossover or spin (slower moves) toward the pivot foot
   - Use the **one-two swing-step maneuver** to keep the potential dribbler under control
   - Arrive as the ball arrives [S15, p.313]
3. Players 3 and X3 play **1-on-1** after a clean reception. Player 3 is allowed to move up and down the sideline, compelling X3 to continually readjust position.

## Coaching Points
- X3 positioning: **two-thirds the distance from player 1, one-third the distance from player 3**. [S15, p.313]
- X3 must be **one step off the line** between players 1 and 3 — sees both players simultaneously.
- X3 must intercept ANY direct pass to player 3.
- On the skip pass closeout: run straight to the receiver, slow down with slide steps the last few steps, overplay the free-foot side.
- "The defender must get to the receiver as the ball arrives." [S15, p.313]
- Player 1's line-drive passes are a test — X3 must maintain interception stance discipline and not drift off.

## Progressions
1. **Intermediate** (base): Line-drive interception practice → lob skip pass closeout → live 1-on-1.
2. **Advanced**: Increase the speed of player 1's fake-out passes; allow player 3 to move along the sideline before the skip pass so X3 must adjust.
