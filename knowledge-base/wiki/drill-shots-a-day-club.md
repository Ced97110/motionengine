# Shots-a-Day Club

## Objective
Develop elite shooting consistency, work ethic, and determination through high-volume daily shooting workouts covering a full range of game-speed shot types.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Full court access (multiple spots around the arc and midrange)
- Tracking chart to record FGA, FGM %, 3PTA, 3PTM %, FTA, FTM %
- Scale workouts down if full volume is too demanding for the athlete's current level

## Execution

### Workout #9-1: 300+ Shots-a-Day Club
Target: **308 total shots** (goal: 60% FG, 60% 3PT, 85% FT)

All shots can come off **one or two dribbles**. Players should alternate directions.

**Shot Fake (SF) Series** — 5 reps each:
1. SF Stride Stop (L)
2. SF Stride Stop (R)
3. SF Stride Stop ST (L) *(step-through)*
4. SF Stride Stop ST (R)
5. SF Big Hop (L)
6. SF Big Hop (R)
7. SF Hop Back 3s (L)
8. SF Hop Back 3s (R)
9. SF HB (L) *(hop back)*
10. SF HB (R)
11. SF HB to Roll Out (L)
12. SF HB to Roll Out (R)
13. SF HB to Cross (L)
14. SF HB to Cross (R)
- **FTs between each set** — 2 reps
- **Game Speed 3PT** — 6×10 = 60 total 3PT shots
- **FTs between each set** — 6×10 = 60 total FTs

**Rip Step Series** — 5 reps each (mirror of SF series):
1. Rip Step Stride Stop (L)
2. Rip Step Stride Stop (R)
3. Rip Step Stride Stop ST (L)
4. Rip Step Stride Stop ST (R)
5. Rip Step Big Hop (L)
6. Rip Step Big Hop (R)
7. Rip Step Hop Back 3s (L)
8. Rip Step Hop Back 3s (R)
9. Rip Step Hop Back (L)
10. Rip Step Hop Back (R)
11. Rip Step HB to Roll Out (L)
12. Rip Step HB to Roll Out (R)
13. Rip Step (L) HB to Cross (L)
14. Rip Step (L) HB to Cross (R)

**Totals:** 308 shots | FG goal: 96–140 (60%) | 3PT goal: 48–80 (60%) | FT goal: 75–88 (85%)

---

### Workout #9-2: 500+ Shots-a-Day Club
Target: **520 total shots** (goal: 60% FG, 60% 3PT, 85% FT)

Same structure as #9-1 but **10 reps per move** (vs. 5):
- Each shot-type set: 10 reps (L) + 10 reps (R)
- FTs between each set: 4 reps
- Game Speed 3PT: 12×10 = 120 total 3PT shots
- FTs between each set: 12×10 = 120 total FTs

**Totals:** 520 shots | FG goal: 120–200 (60%) | 3PT goal: 96–160 (60%) | FT goal: 136–160 (85%)

## Coaching Points
- "All shots must come off game-speed moves — no standing and shooting."
- Alternate left/right on every move to develop ambidexterity
- Track shooting chart every session — percentage trends reveal weaknesses
- If the full workout volume is too demanding, scale it down and build up over weeks
- Free throws between sets serve as active rest AND free-throw conditioning
- The rip step and shot fake series should mirror each other — treat both as equal priorities

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Start at 150–200 shots/day using only spot shots and FTs; add one or two dribble entries when mechanics are sound
2. **Intermediate**: 300+ Club (#9-1) — 5 reps per move, build to 60% FG benchmark
3. **Advanced**: 500+ Club (#9-2) — 10 reps per move, maintain 60% FG and 85% FT benchmarks
