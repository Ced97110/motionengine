# Shot Fake Hop-Back to Crossover Dribble Workout

## Objective
Build the ability to combine a shot fake with a 1- or 2-dribble hop-back and immediately convert into a crossover dribble attack — both left and right — while integrating free-throw shooting between sets to simulate game-fatigue conditions.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court — start from wing, elbow, or top of key
- No additional equipment required
- Three volume tiers: 40 shots (Workout #3-19), 80 shots (Workout #3-20), 120 shots (Workout #3-21)

## Execution

### The Core Move
1. Start in triple-threat position (two-foot jump stop).
2. Execute a **shot fake** (show ball at chin/shooting pocket, feet stay down).
3. Take **1 or 2 dribbles** laterally/backward (hop-back) to create separation.
4. As the defender (or imagined defender) closes out, immediately execute a **crossover dribble** into a driving or pull-up scoring finish.
5. Alternate directions: Left (L) = hop-back to the left, crossover back right; Right (R) = hop-back to the right, crossover back left.

### Workout #3-19 (40 Total Shots)
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 5 reps
- Free Throws: 5
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 5 reps
- Free Throws: 5
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 5 reps
- Free Throws: 5
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 5 reps
- Free Throws: 5
- **Targets:** FG 12-20 (60%), FT 17-20 (85%)

### Workout #3-20 (80 Total Shots)
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 10 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 10 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 10 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 10 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- **Targets:** FG 24-40 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%)

### Workout #3-21 (120 Total Shots)
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 20 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 1 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 20 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (L): 20 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- SF 2 Dribble Hop Back → Crossover (R): 20 reps
- Free Throws: 10
- **Targets:** FG 48-80 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%)

## Coaching Points
- **The shot fake must be convincing** — hold the ball at the shooting pocket with both hands, eyes up at the basket, feet flat. A lazy fake is useless.
- **Hop-back must create real separation** — the player should land in a balanced, shot-ready position even when converting to a crossover.
- **Crossover must be tight** — the dribble hand switches quickly, ball below the knee, shoulder dropped to protect the dribble.
- **Free throws between sets are not rest** — they simulate the late-game free-throw pressure that follows an athletic play. Maintain shooting mechanics.
- **Track FG% and FT% every session** — use the provided workout log to measure progress toward the 60% FG / 85% FT targets.
- Coaching cue: *"Fake it like you mean it — if your feet don't move, neither do his."*

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout #3-19, 40 shots):** 5 reps per set, 1-dribble variations only; focus on shot-fake quality before adding the crossover.
2. **Intermediate (Workout #3-20, 80 shots):** 10 reps per set, both 1- and 2-dribble variations; add a live defender (coach or manager) to make the fake reactive.
3. **Advanced (Workout #3-21, 120 shots):** 20 reps per set; player must read where defender's weight is and choose 1 or 2 dribbles based on gap distance; execute at game speed throughout.
