# Combination Live-Ball Workouts

## Objective
Develop the ability to chain multiple offensive live-ball moves into complete scoring sequences — covering stride stops, step-throughs, big hops, hop-backs, roll-out dribbles, crossover dribbles, and attack layups — through structured, high-volume workout progressions.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court — multiple spots (wing, elbow, top of key, baseline)
- No equipment beyond a ball and basket
- Three levels: Workout #4-1 (130 shots/beginner-intermediate), Workout #4-2 (260+ shots/advanced), and focused workouts for layups (#4-3) and stride stops (#4-4, #4-5)

## Execution

### Core Move Vocabulary (used in all workouts)
- **Stride Stop:** 1-2 stop off the dribble — non-shooting foot lands first (stride), shooting foot follows (stop) — used for pull-up jumpers
- **Step-Through:** After a stride stop, swing the stride foot through the defender to finish at the rim
- **Big Hop:** Explosive two-foot landing off the dribble for a power layup or mid-range shot
- **Hop Back (HB):** Step back off 1 or 2 dribbles to create separation for a jump shot or three
- **Roll-Out Dribble:** Ball rolls to the outside hand off the hop-back to change the attack angle
- **Crossover Dribble:** Ball crosses from one hand to the other to change direction and attack past a closing defender
- **Attack Layup (straight/crossover):** Drive directly to the rim on strong hand (L or R) or cross the defender's body for a reverse/crossover finish

### Workout #4-1: 130 Combination Live-Ball Shots

**Structure:**
- Spot Shots: 5
- FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Stride Stop (L): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos (SF) ½ Dribble Stride Stop (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through (L): 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Big Hop (L): 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Big Hop (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos 1 Dribble Hop Back 3s (L): 5
- Combos 1 Dribble Hop Back 3s (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Hop Back (L): 5
- Combos ½ Dribble Hop Back (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble HB to Roll-Out Dribble (L): 5
- Combos ½ Dribble HB to Roll-Out Dribble (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Combos ½ Dribble HB to Crossover Dribble (L): 5
- Combos ½ Dribble HB to Crossover Dribble (R): 5 → FTs: 5
- Game Speed 3s: 15 (3×5)
- **Targets:** FG 39-65 (60%), 3PT 15-25 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%)

### Workout #4-2: 260+ Combination Live-Ball Shots
*Same sequence as #4-1 but all sets doubled to 10 reps; game speed 3s: 30 (6×5)*
- **Targets:** FG 80-130 (61%), 3PT 30-50 (60%), FT 68-80 (85%)

### Workout #4-3: 55 Combination Live-Ball Attack Layups
- Combos Attack Basket Layups (L): 10 → FTs: 5
- Combos Attack Basket Layups (R): 10 → FTs: 5
- Combos Attack/Crossover Layups (L): 10 → FTs: 5
- Combos Attack/Crossover Layups (R): 10
- **Targets:** FG 38-40 (95%), FT 13-15 (86%)

---

### Workout #4-4: 80 Combination Live-Ball Stride Stop Shots
- Combos 1 Dribble Stride Stop (L): 5 × 2 reps each side (4 sets = 20)
- Combos 2 Dribble Stride Stop (L): 5 × 2 reps each side (4 sets = 20)
- FTs between every set
- **Targets:** FG 24-40 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%)

*Structure detail: 1 Dribble Stride Stop L/R repeated twice; then 2 Dribble Stride Stop L/R repeated twice — 8 sets of 5 shots each = 40 shots; 8 × 5 FTs = 40 FTs.*

---

### Workout #4-5: 120 Combination Live-Ball Stride Stop Shots
*Same structure as #4-4 but 10 shots per set instead of 5.*
- 1 Dribble Stride Stop L/R × 4 sets = 40 shots
- 2 Dribble Stride Stop L/R × 4 sets = 40 shots
- FTs: 5 between each set (8 × 5 = 40 FTs)
- **Targets:** FG 48-80 (60%), FT 34-40 (80%)

## Coaching Points
- **Two-foot jump stop on every catch** — this is non-negotiable for all combination moves. The pivot foot cannot be established without it.
- **Complete the first move before triggering the second** — each move must be sold. A half-executed rip followed by a half-executed shot fake fools nobody.
- **Vary the combination sequence** — don't run the same 2-move sequence every rep. The defender reacts; the combination must adjust.
- **Layup targets are near 95%** — combination layup reps should be finished, not missed. If missing, slow down and focus on the last step.
- **Game-speed 3s at the end of long workouts simulate late-shot-clock decisions** — no rest before these sets.
- Coaching cue: *"Two moves is a conversation. Make the defender answer the first question before you ask the second."*

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout #4-1, 130 shots):** 5 reps per set; execute each combination type in isolation first, then in the full workout sequence.
2. **Intermediate (Workout #4-3 + #4-4):** Layup-specific and stride-stop-specific focused workouts; refine individual finishes before returning to the full combination circuit.
3. **Advanced (Workout #4-2, 260+ shots):** 10 reps per set; add a live/reactive defender for selected sets; player must read and select the combination from a menu, not a script.
