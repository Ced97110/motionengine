# Help and Recover

## Objective
Train the help-side defender (X4) to: (1) maintain proper flat-triangle positioning, (2) hedge toward a penetrating driver to slow them down, (3) recover back to assignment OR rotate and draw the charge, and (4) close out live 1-on-1 when the ball is swung.

## Setup
- Player 3 on strong-side wing with the ball
- Player 4 on strong-side block/corner (X4's assignment)
- Player 1 at the top of the key (passer)
- X4 as the help-side defender
- X3 is imaginary (or another defender guarding player 3)
- Rotation: X4 → 4 → 1 → 3 → X4

<!-- DIAGRAM: Player 3 with ball on right wing, Player 4 on right block/corner, Player 1 at top. X4 in flat triangle help position off player 4. p.296 -->

## Execution
1. Player 3 holds the ball until the coach is satisfied with X4's help-side positioning (flat triangle and ball-you-man).
2. Player 3 begins to drive toward the middle. X4 **hedges** toward player 3 — fakes toward the driver, drops back, fakes again — trying to slow player 3 down so the imaginary X3 can recover.
3. **Option A**: Player 3 drives all the way to the basket — X4 must **rotate** and pick up the charge.
4. **Option B**: At any time, player 3 passes to player 1, who immediately passes to player 4. X4 must **close out** properly on player 4.
5. Player 4 and X4 play live 1-on-1 until X4 rebounds or player 4 scores.

## Options
1. Player 1 starts with the ball and passes to player 3. X4 must begin in denial stance (fence slide) to keep player 4 from receiving. After player 3 catches, they drive → X4 must get into help-side defense, then rotate or recover.

## Key Definitions
- **Hedging**: Defender fakes toward the ball, backs off, fakes again toward the ball, backs off — until the help-side defender controls the dribbler or completely rotates to pick them up.
- **Help and Recover**: Help-side defender hedges, then hurries back to assignment once the dribbler is under control.
- **Rotation**: Help-side defender fully rotates to pick up the breakaway dribbler (leaves assignment).

## Coaching Points
- "Hedge means fake toward them and back off — you're buying time for your teammate to recover"
- Help and recover = hedging + returning to assignment. Rotation = committing to stop the ball.
- X4's hedge must be decisive but quick — a fraction of a second is enough
- Closeout must be under control: break down the last two steps, high hand, low hand
- X4 must read whether X3 has the dribbler under control before deciding to recover or rotate
- "Two-thirds to the ball, one-third to your man — that's your flat triangle"

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: No live play — X4 just practices the hedge and recover sequence without a shot
2. **Intermediate**: Live drive; X4 makes hedge-or-rotate decision; end in live 1-on-1 off the swing pass
3. **Advanced**: Full option with player 1 start, denial stance beginning, and all three outcomes (hedge-recover, rotate-charge, close-out-1v1)
