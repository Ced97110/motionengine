# Flex Offense — Shooting With Three Players

## Objective
Practice the three-player flex action — cutter off the screen, screener popping, and screener rolling to the basket — with live decision-making by the passer.

## Setup
- 3 players: positions 5 (screener), 2 (shooter/cutter), 3 (second cutter/option)
- 2 balls, 2 coach/passers at low-post areas
- Half court

## Execution
1. 5 and 2 each have a ball and pass to the two coaches.
2. 2 moves with 3 for a specific action:
   - **Default**: 3 chooses to exploit 5's vertical screen → 3 pops to the wing; 2 cuts baseline to the opposite corner
   - Coaches can pass to 3 popping, to 2 in the corner, or to 5 who rolls to the basket after screening for 3
3. All three options must be read and executed based on which passing lane is open.
4. Players rotate to the end of the line after shooting/rebounding.

## Coaching Points
- The cutter (2) cutting to the opposite corner must stay low and wide — this pulls the weak-side defender and opens the lane for 5's roll
- The screener (5) must not release from the screen early — set it and hold until 3 clears, then roll hard
- Coaches should vary which option they use so players are reading, not anticipating
- "Roll to the basket with your hands up — you're expecting the pass"

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Predetermined — always pass to the cutter; build muscle memory on the timing
2. **Intermediate**: Coach decides which option to use; players must read and react
3. **Advanced**: Add a defender on 5 to practice screening against resistance and rolling to seal
