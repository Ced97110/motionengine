# 40-Yard Square–Carioca

## Objective
Improve ability to change direction, hip flexibility, and footwork by combining sprint, carioca, backpedal, and reverse pivot movements around a square cone pattern.

## Setup
- 1 player
- 4 cones placed at the corners of a 10 × 10 yard square
- Begin at the bottom-right corner

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance at the bottom-right corner.
2. Sprint forward 10 yards (9 m) along the right side to the first cone. Make a reverse pivot at the cone.
3. Carioca (crossover step) 10 yards (9 m) along the top of the square to the next cone.
4. Reverse pivot and backpedal 10 yards (9 m) down the left side to the next cone.
5. Reverse pivot and carioca 10 yards (9 m) along the bottom of the square back to the start.

## Coaching Points
- The reverse pivot is the key transition movement — plant the foot hard and drive into the next skill
- Carioca sections: maintain hip rotation, keep shoulders square to front
- Backpedal: stay on balls of feet, low center of gravity
- Each corner should be sharp — no rounding of turns
- "New focus point" cue at every corner to reinforce visual habits

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk each leg, master the reverse pivot transition
2. **Intermediate**: Game-tempo execution, timed
3. **Advanced**: Reverse direction (counterclockwise); vary the biomotor skill for each side
