# 40-Yard Lateral Shuffle

## Objective
Improve lateral agility, conditioning, and flexibility in the hip abductors and adductors while developing lateral strength.

## Setup
- 1+ players
- Three parallel lines: starting line, line 5 yards away, line 10 yards away
- Performed entirely in a lateral (side-to-side) direction

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance straddling the starting line.
2. Shuffle 5 yards (4.6 m) to the first line, touch it with the right foot, shuffle back to the starting line, and touch it with the left foot.
3. Shuffle 10 yards (9 m) to the second line, touch it with the right foot, shuffle back to the starting line, and touch it with the left foot.
4. Shuffle 5 yards (4.6 m) to the first line again, touch it with the right foot, and shuffle back to the starting line.

## Coaching Points
- Maintain a defensive-slide-style low stance throughout — do not stand tall between shuffles
- Do NOT cross the feet — true lateral shuffle only
- Touch each line with the correct foot (right foot right line, left foot left line) to reinforce proper deceleration technique
- Keep the chest square to the front — do not rotate shoulders in the direction of movement
- "Stay wide, stay low" coaching cue

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Slow shuffle, emphasis on foot touch accuracy
2. **Intermediate**: Full-speed lateral shuffle, timed
3. **Advanced**: Add a basketball to the shuffle; require a shot fake or live-ball move at each line touch
