# Full Body Pop-Up (Power Pushup Step 9)

## Objective
Develop total-body explosive power by simultaneously launching all four limbs off the floor from a pushup position — the essential prerequisite for the Superman pushup.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Flat floor
- No equipment; padded surface advisable for learning phase

## Execution
1. Squat down, place palms on floor, stretch legs out behind
2. Palms below shoulders at shoulder-width or wider
3. Feet at comfortable width (wider for stability at this demanding level)
4. Keep legs, hips, torso aligned and horizontal
5. Dip arms/shoulders ~1/3 depth
6. Immediately push explosively off floor
7. **Simultaneously kick legs out and up behind you** — keeping body aligned
8. Explode hard enough with all four limbs so **hands AND feet leave the floor at the same time**
9. As gravity reverses, catch floor with both hands and both feet
10. Use elastic rebound to immediately repeat

## Coaching Points
- This requires great trunk strength plus total-body explosiveness — the core must be fully braced to keep body alignment as legs kick up [S9, p.100]
- "This exercise is much easier to do when worming" — but that defeats the purpose; maintain full body alignment as the goal [S9, p.100]
- Regression: pop only **three limbs** off the floor; vary which three (e.g., both hands + one foot; or one hand + both feet) [S9, p.100]
- This step is about the **pattern** (four-limb simultaneous launch) more than upper-body power per se — some earlier steps (convict pushups) are actually more demanding on the upper-body pushing muscles
- This is placed here because it is the mandatory component needed for the Superman [S9, p.100]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Three-limb pop; vary which limbs; get comfortable with mid-air body alignment
2. **Intermediate**: Four-limb simultaneous launch, minimal height
3. **Advanced**: Maximize height and body alignment simultaneously; combine with half-super (Step 8) to build toward Superman Master Step
