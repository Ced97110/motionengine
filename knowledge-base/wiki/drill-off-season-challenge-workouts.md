# Off-Season Challenge Workouts

## Objective
Challenge a player's off-season work ethic and shooting development by accumulating a massive, goal-oriented volume of game-speed shots across the full move menu, tracked with a daily chart.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Full court, half court, or available basket
- Daily tracking chart: Shot Fake Step Fake columns (1–6) + Rip Step columns (1–6) + 3PT column + daily total
- Goal is game-speed execution — 1 or 2 dribbles, alternating directions

## Execution

### Workout #10-1: 10,000+ Off-Season Challenge
Target: **10,070 total shots** including 5,000 free throws

390 reps each of:
1. Shot Fake (SF) Stride Stop
2. SF Stride Stop Step-Through
3. SF Big Hop
4. SF Hop Back
5. SF Hop Back to Roll Out Dribble
6. SF Hop Back to Crossover Dribble
7. Rip Step Stride Stop
8. Rip Step Stride Stop Step-Through
9. Rip Step Big Hop
10. Rip Step Hop Back
11. Rip Step Hop Back to Roll Out Dribble
12. Rip Step Hop Back to Crossover Dribble
13. Rip Step Pull-Up 3PT
- **Free Throws: 5,000**

## Coaching Points
- Coaches should tailor the challenge to fit the needs of their entire team or of individual athletes [S3]
- Charts are critical — athletes must monitor their daily progress to stay motivated and on pace
- "Game-speed" means every rep simulates a real in-game action: off a dribble, proper footwork, real shot intent
- The 3PT challenges count **makes only**, not attempts — this enforces quality over quantity
- Alternating directions (L/R) on every move builds balanced two-way scoring ability
- The 5,000 FTs embedded in the shot challenges maintain free-throw efficiency under fatigue

## Progressions
1. **Entry**: 10,000+ challenge — manageable for a focused off-season of 8–10 weeks
2. **Intermediate**: 15,000+ challenge — serious dedicated player, ~150–200 shots/day over full summer
3. **Elite**: 20,000+ challenge — reserved for the most committed players; requires near-daily gym work
4. **3PT-focused**: Start with 1,000 makes challenge, progress to 4,000 makes over multiple off-seasons
