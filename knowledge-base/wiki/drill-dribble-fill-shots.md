# Dribble Fill Shot Drill Series

## Objective
Train the shooter to read a trailing or low-side closeout off a screen and immediately attack the middle of the paint with a live-ball dribble fill.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter + passer (+ optional live defender)
- **Court area**: Wing and paint, both sides of the floor
- **Equipment**: Stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Screener position**: Near the wing
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start**: Underneath the basket

## Execution
1. Shooter starts underneath the basket and uses the stationary screener to get a wing catch.
2. The defender position (simulated or called by coach) is: **trails the cutter** or closes out on the **baseline/low side**, leaving the middle open.
3. Shooter establishes a **dominant pivot foot** on the catch.
4. Shooter attacks the **middle of the paint** with a dribble fill — purposeful, direct dribbles to scoring range.
5. Shooter uses **any available scoring opportunity**: pull-up mid-range, floater, layup, or draw the foul.
6. Shoot **5 free throws** after each dribble fill set.
7. In the 100-rep version, alternate left and right sides of the floor.

## Coaching Points
- **"Trail or low = middle"** — reinforce the key read: low-side closeout means the middle is open
- **"Establish pivot foot before the dribble"** — no travel risk; set the foot first
- Watch for players over-dribbling — "attack the middle in one or two dribbles, not five"
- Remind players the dribble fill is a **scoring action**, not a pass-it-up drive
- Coach can call "trail" or "high" to create live decision-making reps even with a stationary screener

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-10)**: Single side, 5 dribble fills + 5 FTs × 5 sets. [S3, p.155]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-11)**: Single side. Target: 15/25 (60%), 21/25 FTs (84%). [S3, p.156]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-12)**: Both sides, 5 dribble fills + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each; or 10 dribble fills + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets. Total 150 shots. Target: 60/100 (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.157-161]
