# Mikan Drill for Baby Hook

## Objective
Develop bilateral baby-hook shooting technique and conditioning by continuously shooting left-handed on the left side and right-handed on the right side in a rapid alternating rhythm under the basket.

## Setup
- Players: 1 (individual drill)
- Court area: directly under the basket — paint area
- Equipment: one basketball
- Duration: 1 minute continuous

## Execution
**Beginner:**
1. Player stands under the basket on the **left side** with the ball
2. Shoot a **baby hook with the LEFT hand** from the left side
3. Rebound the made or missed shot
4. **Slide-step** to the other side of the basket
5. Immediately turn back and shoot a **baby hook with the RIGHT hand** from the right side
6. Rebound, slide-step back to the left side
7. Continue for 1 minute without stopping

**Intermediate:**
1. After making the first baby hook, rebound the ball
2. **Jump-stop outside the lane** on the opposite side from where the player started
3. Immediately execute a **drop-step to the middle** and shoot another baby hook
4. Continue side-to-side with the jump-stop and drop-step sequence for 1 minute

**Option (Intermediate only):**
Instead of shooting the baby hook each time, execute a **pump fake** followed by the full pump-fake series (pump fake → crossover, pump fake → up-and-under) — builds post finishing counters in the same conditioning framework

## Coaching Points
- The baby hook is the shot to take when a post player drop-steps to the **middle of the floor** — it is shorter and more protected than a full hook shot [S15, p.181]
- **"Slide-step, don't jog"** — maintain continuous side-to-side lateral movement; no standing still between reps
- Left hand shoots from the left side, right hand shoots from the right side — non-negotiable; develop both hands equally
- Watch for players favoring their dominant hand — enforce bilateral work
- **Jump-stop before the drop-step** (intermediate): the jump stop creates a legal pivot option; the drop-step must be executed immediately without hesitation
- Condition to perform at maximum speed for the full 1-minute duration — this is also a cardio drill

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: continuous baby hooks, slide-step side to side
2. **Intermediate**: add jump-stop outside the lane and drop-step to middle
3. **Intermediate option**: replace baby hook with pump fake series (pump fake crossover or pump fake up-and-under)
4. **Advanced**: add a passive defender who contests every shot; player must read the defender and choose baby hook, pump fake crossover, or up-and-under
