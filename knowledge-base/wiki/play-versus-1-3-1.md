# Versus 1-3-1 (Half-Court Set)

## Overview
A misdirection guard-to-forward set designed to attack the 1-3-1 half-court zone defense. The ball starts on one side and reverses to the other for the attack. The 1-3-1 is designed to keep the offense on the perimeter and eliminate layups — this set counters it by screening the low defender out of position and lobbing to a posting big.

## Formation
- **O2**: Ball handler initiating on the wing
- **O4**: Wing/forward receiving initial pass (ball side)
- **O5**: Posts/screens — relocates to low block hidden from the low defender
- **O3**: Weak-side wing (cutter to ball-side elbow)
- **O1**: Top of key (receives reversal pass, attacks)
- **1-3-1 defenders**: X1 (point), X2/X3/X4 (line), X5 (low baseline)

## Phases

### Phase 1: Initiation and Reversal
- **O2 passes to O4** (strong-side wing) and **cuts through to the opposite corner**
- **O5 screens away for O3** (weak side) and **relocates to the low block** — positioning out of X5's (low zone defender's) vision

### Phase 2: Ball Movement and Cuts
- **O3 cuts from the weak-side wing to the ball-side elbow**, looking for a pass and shot
- **O1 uses a V-cut** to get open at the top of the key for the reversal pass from O4
- **O4 looks at O3** cutting behind the middle defender (X4) — if O3 doesn't receive it, O3 continues to ball-side baseline

### Phase 3: Lob and Triangle Attack
- **O1 catches at the top of the key** and immediately looks to **lob to O5** as O5 steps into the lane behind the defense
- If the lob is unavailable, **O1 takes 1-2 dribbles** and **passes to O2** in the corner
- O2 may **shoot** or **pass inside to O5**
- **O4 relocates to the elbow** and forms a passing triangle with O2 and O5

## Key Coaching Points
- **O5 positioning in the low defender's blind spot** is crucial — if X5 sees O5 dropping, the lob is covered
- **Misdirection**: starting left, attacking right (or vice versa) forces the 1-3-1 to shift before the attack begins
- The **O1→O5 lob** is the primary scoring opportunity; O2's shot or interior pass to O5 is secondary
- The **O3-O4-O5 triangle** created after O4 relocates provides multiple read options
- **"This set offers many opportunities for good shots, and someone is always on the boards."** [S13, p.254]
