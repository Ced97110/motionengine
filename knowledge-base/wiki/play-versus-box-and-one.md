# Versus Box-and-One (Half-Court Set)

## Overview
A modified LA offense designed to attack the box-and-one defense. Rather than trying to free the guarded player (O2) from the man-to-man defender (X2), this set uses O2 as a screener/occupier who pins down a zone defender, allowing O3, O5, and O1 to find open zones and shoot. O2 also has a turnout option off O4's screen if the point guard reverses.

## Formation Setup
- **X2** assigned man-to-man to **O2** throughout
- Players begin in the LA offense (half-court)
- **O1**: PG at wing/ball handler; eventually wing with ball
- **O2**: Guard who is being denied — becomes screener/occupier at ball-side block
- **O3**: Perimeter player (strong-side corner)
- **O4**: Forward (ball-side wing → weak-side block)
- **O5**: High post (center of the floor)

## Phases

### Phase 1: Ball Movement to Attack the Box
- **O1 passes to O3** and receives a **return pass**
- **O1 passes to O2**; O2 **passes to O4**
- **O2 dives to the strong-side block** and **screens X4** (the baseline zone defender)

### Phase 2: Clearing and Repositioning
- **O3 uses X4's screen** (being pinned by O2) and **clears to the strong-side corner**
- **O4 passes to O3** and **clears to the weak-side block**
- **O1 replaces O4** on the wing for the pass
- **O5 remains at the high post** and opens to the ball

### Phase 3: Final Alignment — Passing Triangle
**Final positions:**
- O1: Wing with ball
- O3: Ball-side corner
- O5: High-post elbow ball-side
- O4: Weak-side block
- O2: Ball-side block (screening X4, occupying two defenders simultaneously)

- **O1, O3, and O5 establish a passing triangle** — each looks for an open shot
- **Objective**: find the open holes in the defense; get best shooters to those spots

### Phase 4: Dribble Reversal Option
- If O1 decides to **reverse the ball on the dribble**:
  - **O2 uses O4's screen for a turnout** to the wing
  - **O5 stays at high post** and crosses the free-throw lane to form a triangle with O4 (block) and O2 (wing)

## Key Coaching Points
- **O2 does not fight X2** — instead, O2 screens zone defenders and occupies the defense
- **O2 pinning X4 occupies two defenders** (X2 following O2 + X4 being screened) — a 2-on-2 situation created by one player
- The **passing triangle** (O1-O3-O5) is the scoring mechanism — find the open holes
- **O5 at the high post** is available as a catch-and-shoot or drive option; also as a passer to the corners
- **Do not force the ball to O2** — that's exactly what X2 wants you to do
- **Patience**: this defense will give up open shots if the triangle moves the ball
