# Around-the-World Spinouts

## Objective
Develop consistent catch-and-shoot footwork while moving in both directions around the entire three-point arc, simulating game-speed shots coming off screens.

## Setup
- 1 player (solo drill; can be performed in a group)
- Full three-point arc
- 1 basketball per player
- Starting position: right baseline corner, back to the wall, at the three-point line; player in triple-threat stance

## Execution
1. Begin at the right baseline corner in triple-threat position, facing the court.
2. Spin the ball toward yourself and out onto the court along the three-point line — the ball will bounce off the floor and back toward you, simulating a pass.
3. Run into your shot by planting the **inside pivot foot** (left foot when moving from right to left).
4. Catch the ball, square up, and align yourself to the basket with correct foot positioning and balance.
5. Rise and shoot with proper form — do not sacrifice follow-through for footwork.
6. Freeze the follow-through after each shot.
7. Continue making spinout-catch-shoot repetitions, moving progressively around the three-point arc until you reach the opposite baseline corner.
8. Return to the starting position and repeat in the opposite direction, using the right foot as the inside pivot.

**Key cue:** "Start low, stay low" — maintain an athletic base throughout every repetition.

## Coaching Points
- Players must master footwork **before** adding the shot — begin at half speed, focusing only on footwork; add the shot when squaring up is consistently balanced.
- Watch for players forgetting their follow-through because they're focused on footwork. Remind them: "The goal is swishing the shot with proper form."
- Insist on freezing the follow-through; this reinforces "shoot and stay" for long-rebound tracking.
- "If you don't do it right the first time, when will you do it right?" — hold players to the correct pivot foot on every rep. [S5, p.1]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Perform spinouts around the arc at half speed *without* shooting — focus entirely on inside-foot plant and balanced square-up.
2. **Intermediate**: Add the shot at moderate speed; ensure follow-through is held every rep.
3. **Advanced**: Perform at full game speed; mix direction (start left-to-right one round, right-to-left the next) without pause between reps.
