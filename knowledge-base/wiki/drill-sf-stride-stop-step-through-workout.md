# Shot Fake (SF) Stride Stop / Step-Through Workouts

## Objective
Develop the ability to read the defender off a shot fake — shooting the pull-up if they stay, stepping through if they go airborne — training both responses in all four direction/dribble combinations.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court, midrange areas
- Shot chart for tracking (FGA/FGM, FTA/FTM)

## Execution

### Workout #3-7 (80 total shots — introductory volume)
- SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- (Repeat 1-dribble L/R two more times)
- SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop/Step-Through **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- (Repeat 2-dribble L/R two more times)

**Targets (Workout #3-7):** 24-40 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-8 (120 total shots — intermediate volume)
- Same structure but 10 reps per set, 5 FTs per interval

**Targets:** 48-80 FG (60%), 34-40 FT (80%) [S3]

### Workout #3-9 (240 total shots — advanced volume)
- Same structure but 20 reps per set, 10 FTs per interval

**Targets:** 97-160 FG (60%), 68-80 FT (85%) [S3]

### Per Rep Execution:
1. Start in triple-threat position.
2. Show convincing shot fake.
3. Attack with 1 or 2 dribbles in designated direction.
4. Execute stride stop.
5. **Read decision**: If imagining a defender who stayed → pull up and shoot. If imagining a defender who jumped → step through with a long, low step around the airborne body and finish close.
6. On step-through sets, commit to the finish action every rep to build muscle memory for both options.

## Coaching Points
- "Every shot fake has two reads — know both before you start the move."
- On the step-through: keep the ball low and protected — do NOT swing it high.
- The step-through step is LONG and low — get under the defender's landing zone.
- Alternate step-through sets (some reps pull up, some step through) for full decision training.
- Free throws between every set are non-negotiable.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Isolate the step-through footwork without a dribble; walk through the mechanics, then add the stride stop, then the fake, then the dribble.
2. **Intermediate**: Use workout #3-7 volume (5-rep sets) at deliberate pace.
3. **Advanced**: Use workout #3-9 (20-rep sets, game speed) with a live defender reading the fake.
