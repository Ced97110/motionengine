# Draw the Charge

## Objective
Train the help-side defender to move from proper ball-you-man / flat-triangle positioning into a charge-drawing position before the driver leaves the floor — combining help-side fundamentals with physical courage and correct technique.

## Setup
- Player 1 at the top of the key with the ball
- Player 2 below the foul line extended (help-side position, no ball)
- Player 3 on the weak-side wing (additional offensive player, wait position)
- Rotation: player 2 → player 1 → player 3 → end of line after each charge

## Execution
1. Player 2 rolls the ball to player 1 and immediately hustles to proper help-side defense: ball-you-man and flat triangle positioning.
2. When player 1 receives the ball, player 1 executes a rocker step move, then drives directly to the basket.
3. Player 2 slides over to draw the charge.

## Draw the Charge Technique
1. Player 2 must **stop the slide-step BEFORE player 1 leaves the floor** to shoot the layup — if the feet are still moving on contact, it is a blocking foul.
2. Player 2 **pushes off the balls of the feet** just before contact occurs.
3. Player 2 places **both arms in front of the torso** for protection as contact occurs.
4. Player 2 **lands on the buttocks**.
5. After landing, player 2 **rolls over to the side and raises the top leg** — this prevents injury.

## Coaching Points
- "Get there early — if your feet are moving when they hit you, it's a block"
- Help-side positioning must be precise: two-thirds the distance from the ball, one step off the line between ball and assignment, in interception stance
- Player 2 shifts weight to the other foot as the drive begins (close-the-gap stance)
- Arms in front protect against injury — never reach out
- The rocker step must be executed with quickness (not speed); don't let the attacker rush it
- "Both arms in front of the torso — land on your seat and roll"

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk-through only — player 1 jogs to the basket, player 2 steps in and falls correctly
2. **Intermediate**: Player 1 uses one rocker step move at game quickness
3. **Advanced**: Player 1 uses any rocker step series; player 2 must read the drive and react without being told
