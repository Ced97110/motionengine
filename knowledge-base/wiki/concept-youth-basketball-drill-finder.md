# Youth Basketball Drill Finder (S15 Master Index)

## Summary
The Drill Finder in *Youth Basketball Drills* is a master reference table listing all 160 drills with four key attributes: estimated time (with 10 players + 2 coaches), team vs. individual format, and beginner/intermediate/advanced appropriateness. Coaches use it to rapidly select drills that fit their practice time constraints and squad skill level. [S15, pp.vi–xii]

Time estimates are calibrated to 10 players and 2 coaches; adjust for larger squads or fewer coaches. [S15, p. xviii]

## When to Use
- Use when planning a practice and you have a specific time slot to fill with a drill that matches your players' level
- Use when you want to identify which drills are appropriate for beginners vs. advanced players
- Use when selecting drills that can be run individually (players without a partner) vs. requiring a full team

## Key Principles
1. **Time column is calibrated to 10 players + 2 coaches** — Scale accordingly.
2. **Level markers are cumulative indicators, not exclusive** — A drill marked Beginner + Intermediate is appropriate for both; Advanced only = not for beginners.
3. **Team vs. Individual** — Many drills can be run both ways; individual format enables solo home practice.
