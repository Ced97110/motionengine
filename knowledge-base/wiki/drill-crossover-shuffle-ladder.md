# Crossover Shuffle Agility Ladder Drill

## Objective
Increase hip flexibility and power, and improve ability to change direction, by performing alternating crossover steps through the ladder — only one foot inside the ladder at any time.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Agility ladder laid flat
- Player starts standing with the ladder to their right

## Execution
1. Stand with ladder to the right.
2. **Cross left foot** over the right into the first square.
3. **Laterally step** right foot to the right side (outside) of the ladder.
4. **Immediately cross right foot** over into the second square.
5. **Laterally step** left foot to the left side (outside) of the ladder.
6. Repeat — alternating crossover entry, lateral exit — the full length of the ladder.
7. **Key rule**: Only one foot is inside the ladder at any time.

## Coaching Points
- Drive the crossing foot across forcefully — this is what builds hip power and flexibility
- Keep lateral steps quick and sharp — do not over-step outside
- Stay low; hips should not rise as the foot crosses over
- "Cross and pop" — crossing foot enters, outside foot immediately exits laterally

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Slow walk-through to feel the crossover hip movement before adding speed.
2. **Intermediate**: Game speed; maintain the single-foot-in rule.
3. **Advanced**: Increase the length of the ladder; perform from both starting sides.
