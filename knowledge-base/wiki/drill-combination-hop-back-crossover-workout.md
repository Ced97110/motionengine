# Combination Live-Ball Hop-Back to Crossover Dribble Workout Series

## Objective
Develop both left- and right-hand hop-back to crossover dribble combinations, with built-in free-throw practice between sets to simulate game fatigue and pressure.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Half court / any available basket
- 1 basketball
- Optional: coach or partner to rebound/pass

## Execution

### Workout #4-19 (40 Combination Reps)
1. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left hand) × 5
2. Free throws × 5
3. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right hand) × 5
4. Free throws × 5
5. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left hand) × 5
6. Free throws × 5
7. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right hand) × 5
8. Free throws × 5
- **Targets**: FG 12-20 (60%), FT 17-20 (85%) [S3]

### Workout #4-20 (80 Combination Reps)
1. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left) × 10
2. Free throws × 10
3. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right) × 10
4. Free throws × 10
5. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left) × 10
6. Free throws × 10
7. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right) × 10
8. Free throws × 10
- **Targets**: FG 24-40 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%) [S3]

### Workout #4-21 (120 Combination Reps)
1. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left) × 20
2. Free throws × 10
3. 1 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right) × 20
4. Free throws × 10
5. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Left) × 20
6. Free throws × 10
7. 2 Dribble Hop-Back to Crossover (Right) × 20
8. Free throws × 10
- **Targets**: FG 48-80 (60%), FT 34-40 (85%) [S3]

## Coaching Points
- Stay low throughout the entire combination — "stay in your stance from catch to crossover"
- The hop-back must be convincing; sell the pull-up before crossing over
- Alternate left and right hand sets to build symmetrical skill
- Free throws between sets simulate game pressure and build the mental routine
- Track makes and attempts every session using the built-in shot chart format [S3]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Workout #4-19 (40 reps) — lower volume, build the mechanics first
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #4-20 (80 reps) — double volume, increase pace
3. **Advanced**: Workout #4-21 (120 reps) — full training load at game speed
