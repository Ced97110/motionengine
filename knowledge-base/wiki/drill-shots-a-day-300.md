# 300+ Shots-a-Day Club Daily Workout

## Objective
Build game-speed shot-making consistency across a full menu of scoring moves — shot fakes, rip steps, hop-backs, and three-pointers — while maintaining a 60% FG and 85% FT standard.

## Setup
- 1 player, solo workout
- Full court or half court with access to free-throw line
- 1 basketball, optional rebounder/ball return
- Shot log tracking sheet (Date / FGA / FGM / % / 3PTA / 3PTM / % / FTA / FTM / %)

## Execution

### Structure
All shots come off **one or two dribbles**. Alternate directions (L then R) for every move. Shoot 2 FTs between each move group.

### Left-Column Sequence (Shot Fake Series)
1. Spot Shots — **10 reps**
2. FTs — **2 makes**
3. SF Stride Stop (L) — **5 reps**
4. SF Stride Stop (R) — **5 reps**
5. FTs — **2 makes**
6. SF Stride Stop Step-Through (L) — **5 reps**
7. SF Stride Stop Step-Through (R) — **5 reps**
8. FTs — **2 makes**
9. SF Big Hop (L) — **5 reps**
10. SF Big Hop (R) — **5 reps**
11. FTs — **2 makes**
12. SF Hop Back 3s (L) — **5 reps**
13. SF Hop Back 3s (R) — **5 reps**
14. FTs — **2 makes**
15. SF HB (L) — **5 reps**
16. SF HB (R) — **5 reps**
17. FTs — **2 makes**
18. SF HB to Roll Out (L) — **5 reps**
19. SF HB to Roll Out (R) — **5 reps**
20. FTs — **2 makes**
21. SF HB to Cross (L) — **5 reps**
22. SF HB to Cross (R) — **5 reps**

### Right-Column Sequence (Rip Step Series)
Repeat the identical structure above, replacing Shot Fake moves with Rip Step equivalents:
- Rip Step Stride Stop (L/R) → Rip Step Stride Stop Step-Through (L/R) → Rip Step Big Hop (L/R) → Rip Step Hop Back 3s (L/R) → Rip Step HB (L/R) → Rip Step HB to Roll Out (L/R) → Rip Step HB to Cross (L/R)
- 2 FTs between each pair

### Finish
- Game Speed 3PT: **6 sets × 10 shots = 60 total 3PT shots**
- FTs between each set

## Volume Summary
| Category | Attempts | Make Target | % Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| FG | 140 | 96 | 60% |
| 3PT | 80 | 48 | 60% |
| FT | 88 | 75 | 85% |
| **Total** | **308** | | |

## Coaching Points
- **Every shot must be game speed** — simulate a defender on every rep; no casual catches or lazy pull-ups
- **Alternate left and right** on every dribble-move series; never complete a full set on one side only
- **Freeze the follow-through** on all pull-ups — elbow up, wrist bent, eyes on the target
- **Track every rep** — record makes vs. attempts by category to build accountability and reveal weak spots
- **FTs are not optional** — shoot 2 between every sequence; they simulate late-game pressure situations

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Cut all reps in half (5 spot shots, 2-3 per move, 1 FT between groups); aim for 150 total shots before attempting the full 308
2. **Intermediate**: Full 300+ workout at comfortable pace; focus on hitting the 60/85 percentage benchmarks
3. **Advanced**: Add a second ball retriever or ball return to maintain game-speed pace throughout; push for the 500+ Club volume
