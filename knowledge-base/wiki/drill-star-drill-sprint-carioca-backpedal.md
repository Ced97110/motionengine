# Star Drill — Sprint, Carioca, Backpedal

## Objective
Improve ability to change direction, body position, and transition between sprint, carioca, and backpedal — with emphasis on hip mobility and rotational footwork.

## Setup
- 1 player
- 5 cones in a 10-yard square with one cone in the center (identical to Star Drill #72 setup):
  - Cone 1: start (bottom right)
  - Cone 2: top right
  - Cone 3: top left
  - Cone 4: bottom left
  - Cone 5: center

## Execution
1. Start at cone 1.
2. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 1 to cone 5.
3. **Carioca diagonally** from cone 5 back to cone 1.
4. **Backpedal** from cone 1 to cone 4.
5. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 4 to cone 5.
6. **Carioca** from cone 5 back to cone 4.
7. **Backpedal** from cone 4 to cone 3.
8. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 3 to cone 5.
9. **Carioca** from cone 5 back to cone 3.
10. **Backpedal** from cone 3 to cone 2.
11. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 2 to cone 5.
12. **Carioca** from cone 5 back to cone 2.
13. **Backpedal** from cone 2 to cone 1.

## Coaching Points
- During carioca, rotate hips fully — the trail leg crosses completely in front then behind
- Keep shoulders square (facing sideline) during carioca
- Explode into the sprint immediately after the carioca transition — no hesitation
- "Loose hips" — don't tense up during the carioca, let the hips rotate freely

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Practice carioca in a straight line first before adding the full star pattern.
2. **Intermediate**: Full star pattern at game speed; time each circuit.
3. **Advanced**: Combine with a reactive start (coach signals direction); add ball dribbling during sprint legs.
