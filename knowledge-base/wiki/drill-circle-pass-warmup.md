# Circle Pass Warm-Up Drill

## Objective
Build passing timing, court awareness, and sprint transitions while warming up all five players simultaneously.

## Setup
- 5 players total
- Half court
- Player A (inside arc) is under the basket; Player A (outside arc) is at the top of the key
- Players B and D are on the left and right sides inside the arc
- Corresponding B and D players are outside the arc
- No equipment other than a basketball for each "inner" player

## Execution
1. Drill starts: A inside the arc passes to B on his right; simultaneously, A outside the arc passes to B on his right.
2. Both As immediately sprint to exchange positions (inside A sprints outside; outside A sprints inside).
3. Continue passing to the right — B passes to C, C to D — each time the passer sprints to exchange positions with his counterpart.
4. Run for a set number of passes or a set time.

## Coaching Points
- Passes and exchanges happen simultaneously — neither waits for the other
- Sprint exchanges, not jogs
- Passers must deliver on time so the receiver catches in rhythm
- Watch for lazy exchanges — require full sprint effort
- "Make crisp passes on time, then sprint!" [S7, p.306]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner:** Walk through the passing sequence first, then add sprints
2. **Intermediate:** Add a second ball for double the passing volume
3. **Advanced:** Increase speed requirement; coach sets a pass-count target in a fixed time window
