# Wing to Top Rip Step Live-Ball Move Shooting (Workout #11-18)

## Objective
Isolate and reinforce the **rip step live-ball move** — catching beyond the three-point line, ripping through to create drive space, and finishing with a pull-up or step-through shot — within the wing-to-top movement pattern.

## Setup
- 1 player (rebounder/passer recommended)
- Half court, three-point arc
- Same 3-spot wing-to-top movement pattern
- Shot chart tracking FGA, FGM, %, FTA, FTM, %
- **Total shots**: 80 per full session (60 field goal attempts + 20 free throws)

## Execution
1. Player moves through the **wing → top → wing** arc in the same pattern as Workouts #11-16 and #11-17.
2. At each spot, the player **catches on a two-foot stop** past the three-point line.
3. Upon catching, the player executes a **rip step live-ball move** — ripping the ball from one side of the body to the other (low, tight to the body) to create a driving or shooting advantage past the three-point line.
4. After the rip step, the player attacks or rises for a shot.
5. Repeat until **10 shots** have been taken per set.
6. Full session structure:
   - Right Side: Rip Step Shots (10) → FTs (5) → Rip Step Shots (10) → FTs (5) → Rip Step Shots (10)
   - Left Side: same structure
7. All moves and shots at **game speed**.

## Coaching Points
- The **rip step** is a low, swinging motion — keep the ball protected below the defender's hands, not a wide high arc.
- After the rip, the player's momentum should be loaded to drive; if the defender doesn't react, rise immediately for the shot.
- "Rip low, explode high" — the rip creates a physical and visual threat that opens the shot.
- Benchmark: FG 36-60 (60%), FT 17-20 (85%).
- Both sides of the floor must be trained — develop the rip step going left *and* right.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Execute the rip step without a defender, focusing on keeping the ball low and tight.
2. **Intermediate**: Add a live passer who delivers the ball at varying angles — forces the player to adjust the rip to the catch angle.
3. **Advanced**: Add a live defending hand in the passing lane; player must protect the catch and immediately rip through.
