# Scissor Jumps

## Objective
Improve quickness in the hips and enhance balance through alternating explosive leg kicks. [S8, p.168]

## Setup
- 1 player
- Open floor or field
- No equipment

## Execution
1. Begin by kicking one leg forward and up into the air as if punting a football.
2. While the first leg is in the air, leave the ground with the opposing leg and repeat the kick with the opposite leg while the initial leg is returning to the ground.
3. Continue the alternating scissor pattern as quickly as possible for a predetermined number of reps or for time.
4. Ensure equal work initiating the drill with both legs.

## Coaching Points
- Arms should drive in opposition to the legs to assist balance and rhythm.
- "Fast scissors" — the speed of leg exchange is the training variable.
- Equalize reps starting with each leg — hip asymmetries are common and this drill can reveal them.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Slow controlled alternating kicks; focus on balance.
2. **Intermediate**: Increase kick tempo; maintain upright posture.
3. **Advanced**: Maximum speed for 10-second bursts; increase to 15–20 second bursts.
