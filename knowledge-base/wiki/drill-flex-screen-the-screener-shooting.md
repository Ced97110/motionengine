# Flex Offense Screen-the-Screener Shooting Drill (4-on-4)

## Objective
Practice the flex offense's signature screen-the-screener sequence, skip pass continuity, and dual-side execution against live defenders.

## Setup
- 4 offensive players vs. 4 defenders (half court)
- 1 at the top of the key with the ball
- 3 at the wing, 4 at the elbow/high post, 2 weakside
- Two balls can be used for high-rep versions (coaches as passers)

## Execution
**Phase 1:**
1. **1** passes to **3**
2. When the ball reaches 3's hands, **4** makes a back screen for **2**
3. Immediately after screening for 2, **4** receives a high screen from **1** (screen-the-screener action)
4. **4** goes high off 1's screen and receives the ball from **3**

**Phase 2 (Continuity):**
5. **4** makes a skip pass to **1**
6. When the ball is in 1's hands, **2** screens for **3**
7. **4** screens **2** (screen-the-screener)
8. Continue until no effective shooting situation can be found
9. Rotate offense and defense; run on both sides of the floor

## Coaching Points
- Screener timing: 4's back screen for 2 must be set BEFORE the ball leaves 3's hands
- "4, screen and immediately use the screen — no hesitation"
- 1's high screen for 4 must be a legal stationary screen at the elbow
- Skip pass from 4 to 1 must be sharp and accurate — skip passes are the triggers for the next action
- Defenders: communicate early — "screen left!" before the screen is set
- Play continues live — players learn to read when to shoot vs. continue

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk-through, no defenders, coach calls each action
2. **Intermediate**: Add defenders on the two primary cutters only
3. **Advanced**: Full 4-on-4 live with score tracking; losers run
