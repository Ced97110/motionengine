# Half-Super Pushup (Power Pushup Step 8)

## Objective
Develop extreme shoulder speed by thrusting both hands forward beyond the head during airtime — mastering the upper-limb component of the Superman pushup.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Flat floor
- No equipment

## Execution
1. Squat down, place palms on floor, stretch legs out behind
2. Palms below shoulders at shoulder-width or wider
3. Feet at comfortable width
4. Keep legs, hips, torso aligned and horizontal
5. Dip ~1/3 depth
6. Immediately push explosively off floor
7. In mid-air, **thrust both hands directly out in front of the body** — beyond and past the head
8. Return hands in front to catch floor before impact
9. Use elastic rebound to immediately repeat

## Coaching Points
- Named "half-super" because the upper half of the body achieves the Superman flying position [S9, p.98]
- Builds **high levels of pure shoulder speed** — directly useful for any explosive forward arm action [S9, p.98]
- This step and Step 9 (full body pop-up) are both prerequisites for the Superman; master both independently before combining
- Regression: begin with **one hand out front at a time** on each side; when easy bilaterally, attempt full version [S9, p.98]
- Kneeling version can also be explored as preparation
- Advanced progression: on the return journey of the arms (before catching the floor), **clap the top of the head** — adds another fast-twitch demand [S9, p.99]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: One arm forward at a time, alternating sides; kneeling version
2. **Intermediate**: Both arms out front simultaneously; full half-super
3. **Advanced**: Add head-clap on arm return journey; combine with full body pop-up to build toward Superman
