# Medicine-Ball Release Push-Ups With Partner

## Objective
Improve quickness in the upper-body pushing musculature through a throw-and-catch sequence integrated with explosive push-up mechanics. [S8, p.158]

## Setup
- 2 players facing each other at appropriate distance
- Equipment: 1 medicine ball

## Execution
1. Starting athlete kneels and holds the medicine ball at chest height.
2. Throw the medicine ball explosively to the partner, then immediately fall forward into a push-up position.
3. Push up explosively back to the start/kneeling position while the partner returns the medicine ball.
4. Catch the returned ball and repeat the throw-push-up sequence.
5. Perform as quickly as possible — minimize time between throw and push-up, and between push-up and catch.

## Coaching Points
- Timing is critical — the athlete must be ready to catch upon returning to the start position.
- "Throw and fall" — no hesitation between the release and the push-up drop.
- Keep the body straight throughout the push-up phase; no hip sag.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Practice the throw and the push-up separately before combining.
2. **Intermediate**: Execute the combined sequence at game speed.
3. **Advanced**: Increase ball weight; increase distance between partners; switch to one-arm throws.
