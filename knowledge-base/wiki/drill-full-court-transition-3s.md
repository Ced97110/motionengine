# Full-Court Transition 3s (Workouts #6-5 & #6-6)

## Objective
Develop the ability to shoot accurate three-point shots off full-court sprints, building both shooting rhythm under fatigue and elite cardiovascular conditioning. [S3, p.167]

## Setup
- 1 shooter (rebounder/passer optional)
- Full court
- No equipment needed
- Passer (if used) rebounds and feeds shooter on the move

## Execution

### Workout #6-5: 25 Full-Court Transition 3s
1. Shooter starts on the **right side of the floor at half court**.
2. Sprints to the three-point line and **rhythms up** into a three-point shot.
3. After the shot: sprints and **touches the baseline** underneath the basket.
4. Sprints up the **opposite side** of the floor to half court.
5. Repeats the pattern, alternating sides.
6. Continue until shooter has taken **5 three-point shots from each side**.
7. Also includes middle/top-of-key shots.

**Sequence:**
- Full Court 3PT (Right) × 5
- Full Court 3PT (Left) × 5
- FTs × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Right) × 5
- Full Court 3PT (Left) × 5
- FTs × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Middle) × 5
- FTs × 5

**Total: 50 shots (25 three-pointers + 25 free throws)**

**Standards:** 3PT: 15-25 (60%); FT: 22-25 (88%) [S3, p.168]

---

### Workout #6-6: 50 Full-Court Transition 3s
Same pattern as #6-5 but **doubles the volume** of three-pointers at each station.

**Sequence:**
- Full Court 3PT (Right) × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Left) × 10
- FTs × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Right) × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Left) × 10
- FTs × 10
- Full Court 3PT (Middle) × 10
- FTs × 10

**Total: 80 shots (50 three-pointers + 30 free throws)**

**Standards:** 3PT: 30-50 (60%); FT: 17-30 (85%) [S3, p.169]

## Coaching Points
- **Rhythm up**: the shooter must arrive at the three-point line in a rhythmic stride — not a dead stop. The last step should be an inside-foot plant leading into a balanced shot.
- **Touch the baseline**: this is non-negotiable — it ensures the conditioning demand is met and simulates the full defensive transition distance.
- **Sprint the opposite lane**: do not drift to the middle; maintain lane discipline to simulate game-speed outlet spacing.
- **Free throws between sets**: simulate late-game pressure free throws taken on fatigued legs — don't treat them as rest.
- Coaching cue: *"Sprint, rhythm, release — no drifting, no coasting."*

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: 10 total transition 3s per session; allow 75% sprint effort; passer at the basket.
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #6-5 (25 shots) at full effort.
3. **Advanced**: Workout #6-6 (50 shots) — double volume demands elite conditioning and mental focus under fatigue.
