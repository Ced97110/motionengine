# Dribble Fill Off a Screen Drill

## Objective
Train the cutter to read a trailing or bodying defender who closes on the baseline side, then attack the middle of the paint with a dribble fill and execute any scoring opportunity.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter (cutter), passer, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Court area**: Half court — wing and paint
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Screener position**: Wing or elbow
- **Equipment**: Basketballs, optional cones/chairs

## Execution
1. Shooter begins underneath the basket and sets up the screen with a V-cut
2. Shooter uses the stationary screener and fills to the wing to receive the pass
3. The drill simulates the defender **trailing or bodying** the cutter and closing out on the **baseline or low side**, leaving the middle open
4. Shooter catches on the wing and immediately establishes a dominant pivot foot
5. Shooter attacks the middle of the paint with a dribble fill
6. Any scoring opportunity is taken once the middle has been attacked — floater, pull-up, layup, or kick-out
7. After the scoring attempt, shooter shoots 5 free throws, then repeats

## Coaching Points
- "If they take the baseline, take the middle" — dribble fill is the counter to the dribble fade
- Establish the dominant pivot foot on the catch before driving
- Attack with purpose — dribble fill is a strong, decisive drive to the paint, not a drift
- Any scoring opportunity is valid: floater, pull-up jumper, layup, or draw foul
- Contrast with the fade: this is an inside attack, not a retreat to the baseline
- Compare to dribble curl: fill starts from a wider catch; curl starts from a tighter cut angle toward the elbow

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-10)**: 5 dribble fills + 5 FTs × 5 sets. One side of floor. [S3, p.148]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-11)**: 15–25 fills made (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). One side of floor. [S3, p.149]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-12)**: Split left/right — 5 dribble fills + 5 FTs × 5 sets per side. Targets: 30–50 fills (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.149-150]
4. **Elite (150 shots — Workout #5-12 extended)**: 10 dribble fills + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 60–100 fills (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.151]
