# 500+ Shots-a-Day Club Daily Workout

## Objective
Achieve elite-level daily shot volume (520 shots) across the complete SF and Rip Step scoring-move taxonomy, building shot-making automaticity at game speed.

## Setup
- 1 player, solo workout (ball return machine or partner rebounder recommended at this volume)
- Full or half court
- Shot log tracking sheet

## Execution

### Structure
Identical move taxonomy to the 300+ Club, but **all reps doubled**: spot shots increase from 10 to 20, each dribble-move series from 5 to 10, FTs between sets from 2 to 4.

### Shot Fake (SF) Series — 10 reps per direction
1. Spot Shots — **20 reps**
2. FTs — **4 makes**
3. SF Stride Stop L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
4. SF Stride Stop Step-Through L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
5. SF Big Hop L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
6. SF Hop Back 3s L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
7. SF HB L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
8. SF HB to Roll Out L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4
9. SF HB to Cross L/R — **10 each**; FTs — 4

### Rip Step Series — 10 reps per direction
Mirror the SF series exactly with Rip Step equivalents:
- Rip Step Stride Stop, Stride Stop Step-Through, Big Hop, Hop Back 3s, HB, HB to Roll Out, HB to Cross
- 4 FTs between each pair

### Finish
- Game Speed 3PT: **12 sets × 10 shots = 120 total 3PT shots**
- 4 FTs between each set

## Volume Summary
| Category | Attempts | Make Target | % Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| FG | 200 | 120 | 60% |
| 3PT | 160 | 96 | 60% |
| FT | 160 | 136 | 85% |
| **Total** | **520** | | |

## Coaching Points
- **Ball security on the catch** — at this volume, fatigue causes sloppy catches; maintain fingertip control throughout
- **Maintain shot mechanics under fatigue** — if form breaks down, take a brief rest rather than ingraining bad habits
- **Use the FT sets as mindfulness resets** — after a string of field goals, shift focus entirely to the free-throw routine
- **Log every session** — progressive tracking (FGA, FGM, 3PTA, 3PTM, FTA, FTM) reveals trends over weeks and months

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Complete the 300+ Club first; add 5 reps per move category weekly until reaching 500+ volume
2. **Intermediate**: Full 500+ Club at moderate pace; target percentage benchmarks before increasing speed
3. **Advanced**: Full 500+ Club at game speed with a live passer/ball return; complete in under 90 minutes
