# Fratello 3-on-3 Help-and-Recover / Full-Rotation Drill

## Objective
Develop three-player defensive communication, help-and-recover rotations, and full-rotation trapping in response to baseline drives and middle penetration.

## Setup
- Offensive players 1, 2, 3 on both wings and at the top of the key
- Three defenders (X1, X2, X3) matched to each offensive player
- Ball can start at any of the three positions
- Half-court; play is live
- Make the drill competitive by keeping score; always send the next three on defense, not offense

## Execution

### Baseline-Drive Rotation
1. Ball starts on the right wing (1). X1 guards 1 and **forces him to drive to the baseline**.
2. X3 rotates off 3 and cuts off 1's baseline drive.
3. X2 rotates off 2 and picks up 3 on the baseline.
4. X1 rotates off the baseline back to 2 at the top of the key.
5. Play is live until the ball is secured by the defense or the offense scores. [S7, p.239]

### Middle Penetration — Help-and-Recover
1. Ball is forced to the middle of the court.
2. X2 steps in to help stop 1's middle penetration and recovers to 2.
3. X1 stays with 1.
4. X3 positions to help X2 on 2's penetration, then recovers to 3. [S7, p.239]

### Middle Penetration — Full Rotation
1. 1 penetrates to the middle of the court.
2. X2 traps the ball.
3. X3 rotates to 2.
4. X1 rotates to 3. [S7, p.239]

## Coaching Points
- Every rotation must be preceded by verbal communication: "Baseline!" / "I've got the trap!" / "Rotate!"
- **Help-and-recover** = nearest helper stunts and returns to his man.
- **Full rotation** = nearest helper fully leaves his man and traps; next defender covers the open man — more aggressive but leaves someone free.
- "These drills are designed to help with communication and quick reaction in help-and-recover and full-rotation situations." [S7, p.239]
- Keep score to build competitive intensity; "always send the next three in on defense, not offense."

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through the baseline-drive rotation only; no live play.
2. **Intermediate**: Help-and-recover live; full rotation pre-called.
3. **Advanced**: Both rotations live; offense reads the rotation and makes the right pass.
