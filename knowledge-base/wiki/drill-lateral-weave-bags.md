# Lateral Weave (Bag Drill)

## Objective
Improve quickness of foot movements and reaction time by performing rapid lateral sidesteps through 3-4 bags with an immediate direction reversal before sprinting forward. [S8, p.117]

## Setup
- 1 player
- 3-4 agility bags in a straight lateral line, spaced approximately 2-3 feet apart
- 5 yards of open space on one end for the sprint finish

## Execution
1. Start in a two-point stance with hands and arms away from the body.
2. Sidestep laterally over all three (or four) bags as quickly as possible, moving to the right or left.
3. After crossing the last bag, **immediately reverse direction** and sidestep back across all bags the other way.
4. Once you cross the last bag on the return trip, **sprint forward 5 yards (4.6 meters)**. [S8, p.117]

## Coaching Points
- "Immediately" means no hesitation — the reversal is the hardest moment; train it to be reflexive
- Keep hands and arms out for lateral balance
- Do not cross feet during sidestepping
- Stay low in athletic stance throughout — the sprint at the end rewards players who stayed ready
- Head up — simulate reading an opponent while moving laterally

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: 3 bags, slower pace — learn the reversal pattern
2. **Intermediate**: 4 bags at full speed
3. **Advanced**: React to a visual or verbal cue from a coach for the reversal and sprint direction
