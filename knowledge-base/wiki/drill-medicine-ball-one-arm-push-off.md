# Medicine-Ball One-Arm Push-Off

## Objective
Improve power and quickness in the upper-body pushing musculature through unilateral explosive push-ups with one hand elevated on a medicine ball. [S8, p.163]

## Setup
- 1 player
- Flat floor
- Equipment: 1 medicine ball

## Execution
1. Begin in a push-up position with body straight, one hand on the medicine ball and one hand on the floor.
2. Drop down into a push-up position.
3. Explosively push off with the hand that is on the ground — this hand leaves the ground until it reaches the height of the medicine ball or just above it.
4. The hand on the ball stays on the ball throughout the entire exercise.
5. Lower the pushing hand back to the ground in a controlled manner.
6. Upon the hand's contact with the ground, immediately deliver the next explosive push.
7. Goal: spend the least amount of time possible in contact with the ground.

## Coaching Points
- "Off the ground fast" — the training stimulus is the explosive push from the floor-side hand.
- The controlled lowering phase is important; do not drop or collapse.
- Equal work on both sides — switch ball side regularly.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Master plyo push-ups (bilateral) before attempting this unilateral version.
2. **Intermediate**: Perform for timed sets; count reps per 10 seconds.
3. **Advanced**: Increase ball height (stack balls) or add lateral transitions between reps.
