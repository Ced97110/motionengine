# Over-and-Under Drill

## Objective
Simulate game-specific jump shots from corner and wing positions using sprint exchanges and live passes from two coaches.

## Setup
- Half court
- Two coaches, each with a ball: one outside the baseline (corner area), one in the central lane near the foul line
- Two rebounders in the lane
- Player 1 starts in the corner; Player 2 starts on the wing

## Execution
1. On cue, Player 1 (corner) and Player 2 (wing) sprint and exchange positions.
2. As they exchange, each receives a pass from the nearest coach and immediately takes a jump shot from their new location.
3. Rebounders retrieve and return balls to coaches.
4. Repeat for a set number of shots or a set time, then run on the other side.

## Coaching Points
- Players are always moving — no standing; sprint the exchange
- Feet must be set before the shot — the exchange sprint leads into a balanced landing, not a rushed one-foot shot
- "Perfect feet, perfect follow-through" on every rep
- Use game-speed catches — hands up, knees bent, ready before the ball arrives
- Coaches time the pass to arrive as the player lands in position [S7, p.307]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner:** Slow jog on exchanges; focus purely on footwork and follow-through
2. **Intermediate:** Full-speed exchanges; coach varies the timing of the pass (early, late) to force adjustment
3. **Advanced:** Add a passive defender on one shooter to practice shooting over a hand
