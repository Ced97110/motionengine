# 1-on-1 Team Drill (#106)

## Objective
Teach all perimeter live-ball fakes and dribbling moves against a live defender who cannot move until the offensive player initiates — builds triple-threat reads in a controlled setting.

## Setup
- **Players**: 2 per group (attacker 1, defender X1); multiple groups run simultaneously at three different half-court positions (top of key, left wing, right wing)
- **Court area**: Half court; three simultaneous stations shown in the diagram
- **Equipment**: 1 basketball per group
- **Duration**: 1 minute per role, then switch offense/defense

<!-- DIAGRAM: Three simultaneous 1v1 stations — one at top of key, one at each wing — each with attacker and defender face-to-face. Arrows show dribble drives and defensive reactions. -->

## Execution
1. Players line up in three groups; one pair per station on the half court.
2. Player 1 begins with the basketball and may use any fake desired from triple-threat position.
3. **Teaching progression**:
   - Phase 1: No dribble — only fakes and footwork
   - Phase 2: One dribble allowed
   - Phase 3: Two dribbles allowed
   - Phase 4: Attack in either direction
4. Player 1 may shoot only if X1 is off balance — do not force shots.
5. X1 begins in **front foot to pivot foot** defensive stance.
6. **X1 cannot move until player 1 moves** — reactive defense only.
7. After 1 minute, offense and defense switch roles.

## Coaching Points
- X1 must wait for player 1 to move before reacting — this teaches true defensive reactivity
- "Front foot to pivot foot" — defender's lead foot matches attacker's pivot foot
- Progress slowly through the dribble phases; don't advance until technique is sound
- Use the coach-behind-X1 option (holding up numbers) to dictate which fakes/dribbles to use without the defender knowing
- Alternate pivot feet from rep to rep to develop bilateral footwork

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: No dribble; fakes and footwork only; attack one direction only
2. **Intermediate**: 1-2 dribbles; attack in either direction
3. **Advanced**: Any move with multiple dribbles; coach holds up numbers behind defender to signal specific moves; player 1 alternates right/left pivot foot each rep; Option: player stands under the basket executing pump fakes and pump-fake crossovers as if at end of dribble
