# Indiana Pacers Box Set — High X Action

## Overview
Larry Bird's 1997-98 Indiana Pacers (NBA Coach of the Year) used a 1-2-2 box set to generate scoring opportunities for Reggie Miller (2) and Jalen Rose (3) off baseline screens — and used the threat of those shooters to set up post opportunities for Rik Smits (5) and Dale Davis (4). The "High X" involves the two post players crossing in the high post — one cuts to the block while the other sets a down screen — creating a post feed or a top-of-key jump shot depending on ball direction. [S14, pp.63-65]

## Formation
- **1 (PG, Mark Jackson)** at the top
- **2 (SG, Reggie Miller)** on the right low block
- **3 (SF, Jalen Rose / Chris Mullin)** on the left low block
- **4 (PF, Dale Davis)** at the right elbow
- **5 (C, Rik Smits)** at the left elbow

## Phases

### Phase 1: PG Dribbles to Initiate — High X Cross
- 1 (Mark Jackson) dribbles to the **left wing**
- As 1 dribbles past 5, both post players begin the **High X**:
  - **4** cuts to the **left block** (post-up)
  - **5** sets a **down screen for 2** (who cuts to the top)
- First option: **post-feed to 4** on the left block
- Second option: **pass to 2** cutting to the top off 5's screen
- 3 times his cut to move off 5 out to the **right corner**
- 5 then posts up on the **right block** after screening

<!-- DIAGRAM: Fig. 6.16 — 1 dribbles left wing; 4 cuts left block; 5 down-screens for 2; 3 clears to right corner; 5 posts right block. -->

### Phase 2: Ball Reversal to Post Smits
- If 2 receives the pass at the top and is not open for a shot:
- 2 swings the ball to 3 in the right corner
- 3 looks to make the **low post-feed to 5** (Smits) on the right block

<!-- DIAGRAM: Fig. 6.17 — 2 swings to 3 in right corner; 3 post-feeds Smits (5) on right block. -->

### Phase 3: Mirror Action (Opposite Side)
- Same action run to the right side of the floor:
  - 5 (Smits) cuts to the **right block** for the initial post look
  - 4 sets a down screen to bring **Mullin/Miller** to the top
  - 2 cuts off 4 to the **left corner**
  - Same post-feed and ball-reversal options exist

<!-- DIAGRAM: Fig. 6.18 — Mirror: 5 goes right block; 4 down-screens for top shooter; 2 clears left corner. -->

## Key Coaching Points
- The High X must be read by the PG based on which direction he dribbles — the play self-organizes from ball movement
- "This combination play zeros in on either shooter, depending on the direction the 1 takes his dribble" [S14, p.65]
- Miller and Rose's shooting threat is what creates the post opportunities for Smits and Davis — the inside-outside connection is the strategic core
- Bird ran this action specifically looking to reverse the ball to post up Smits on the right block
- 3 must time his corner clear to coincide with 5 screening for 2 — they cannot both be at the top simultaneously

## Counters
- If the post-feed to 4 is denied → pass to 2 at the top off 5's screen (second option is automatic)
- If 2 is denied at the top → reverse to 3 in the corner for the Smits post-feed
- Run to the right side (Fig. 6.18) when the defense is rotating to cover the left-side initial action
