# Superman (Rebounding Drill)

## Objective
Condition players to pursue the second, third, and fourth rebound without stopping — building explosive lateral leap ability, tipping technique, and relentless effort under fatigue.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 basketball, a basket with a backboard
- Player starts on a big block near the basket
- Begin with 30-second sessions; build up to a full 1 minute by end of season

## Execution

### Beginner
1. Player with a basketball stands on a big block near the basket.
2. **Toss the ball off the backboard** so it caroms to the other side of the basket.
3. **Slide-step** across the lane to the other side, **leap** into the air, and **grab the rebound**. Turn the body in the air while coming down.
4. **Immediately** toss the ball off the board again so it caroms back to the original side.
5. Slide-step back, leap, grab the rebound, turn in the air, land.
6. Continue non-stop for the full time interval.
7. Start with **30-second sessions**; work up to a **full minute** by end of season.

### Intermediate (Lid Variation)
1. Place a **lid over the basket** so the ball rolls off when it lands on it.
2. Instead of grabbing and tossing, the player **tips the rebound**, lands, tips again, lands, tips again.
3. Continue for 30 seconds — all tips, no catching.

## Coaching Points
- Check that the player's body positioning from **toes to arm tips** matches perfect rebounding form (see [[drill-bull-in-ring-rebounding]] body positioning points).
- **No resting between rebounds** — the drill is specifically designed to condition pursuit of the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th rebound opportunity.
- Tips should be executed with **fingertip control** — no slapping or palming.
- The turn in the air while landing teaches body control and position for outlet passes or pump fakes.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Catch and toss; 30 seconds; coach monitors form.
2. **Intermediate**: Lid over the basket; continuous tips without catching; 30 seconds.
3. **Advanced**: Full 1-minute sessions; increase toss distance; add pump fake before re-toss.

- [[concept-offensive-rebounding-techniques]] — airtime body control and landing balance
