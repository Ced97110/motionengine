# Spot Shot Workout Series (Workouts #11-2 and #11-3)

## Objective
Build full-speed outside shooting efficiency by combining high-volume spot shots with free throws in a solo, self-rebounding, conditioning workout format.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Full court / half court
- No equipment beyond the ball
- **No shots inside 5 feet are allowed**
- Three-point shots are permitted

## Execution

### Workout #11-2: 50 Spot Shots
Total shots: 50 (25 spot shots + 25 FTs). Alternate sets of 5:
1. Shooter starts directly under the basket and throws the ball out toward the center of the court.
2. Shooter sprints to catch up to the ball, squares up, and shoots — outside only (minimum 5 feet).
3. Immediately sprint after the rebound; **do not walk**.
4. Throw the ball out again and repeat.
5. After every 5 spot shots, shoot 5 free throws at the line.
6. Repeat the cycle 5 times:
   - Spot Shots × 5 → FTs × 5 (repeat 5 rounds = 25 spot shots + 25 FTs)

**Target benchmarks:** Spot shots 15–25 (60%); FTs 21–25 (84%)

### Workout #11-3: 100 Spot Shots
Total shots: 125 (100 spot shots + 25 FTs). Same mechanics as above but sets of 20:
1. Same self-pass and sprint technique throughout.
2. After every 20 spot shots, shoot 5 free throws.
3. Repeat 5 times:
   - Spot Shots × 20 → FTs × 5 (repeat 5 rounds = 100 spot shots + 25 FTs)

**Target benchmarks:** Spot shots 60–100 (60%); FTs 21–25 (84%)

## Coaching Points
- "Sprint after every rebound — this is a conditioning drill, not a walking drill."
- The self-pass throw simulates catching a pass on the move — square up before shooting.
- Maintain consistent mechanics even when fatigued; this is where it counts.
- Track makes and attempts on a shot chart after each session.
- No shots inside 5 feet — this is a perimeter shooting workout.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout #11-2):** 50 total shots, sets of 5 spot shots + 5 FTs × 5 rounds.
2. **Intermediate (Workout #11-3):** 125 total shots, sets of 20 spot shots + 5 FTs × 5 rounds.
3. **Advanced:** Incorporate live-ball moves before each spot shot (see ).
