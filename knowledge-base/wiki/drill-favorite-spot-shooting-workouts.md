# Favorite Spot Shooting Workouts

## Objective
Build shooting confidence and ingrain correct mechanics at a player's strongest court locations by using a make-based quota system — the drill does not end until the shooter reaches the target number of makes.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court
- Player pre-selects **3 spots** on the floor where they have the most success making shots
- Self-pass: throw ball to self, sprint to ball, square up, shoot
- Sprint after every rebound — do not walk

## Execution
1. Before beginning, the player identifies and commits to 3 specific spots on the floor where they are most successful.
2. Starting at Spot 1, the player throws the ball out, sprints to it, squares up, and shoots.
3. The player chases every rebound at full speed.
4. The player shoots from Spot 1 until they reach the make quota for the workout (10, 20, or 30 — see variants).
5. It does not matter how many attempts it takes — only makes count.
6. Once the make quota is reached at Spot 1, move to Spot 2. Then Spot 3.

## Coaching Points
- **Make-based, not attempt-based.** The quota is makes — this forces players to stay locked in and maintain mechanics regardless of a bad stretch.
- **Spot selection matters.** Coaches should verify that spots are realistic game spots (e.g., corner three, elbow, wing) — not just layup range.
- **Sprint every rep.** Simulates getting to a spot after a cut or off-ball movement.
- **Confidence first.** Starting with favorite spots allows players to ingrain correct mechanics with high success — before moving to weaker spots.
- Track FGA, FGM, and % per spot per date for long-term measurement.

## Common Mistakes
1. Choosing spots too close to the basket → correction: keep all spots at game-realistic distances (minimum 5 feet)
2. Walking to rebounds → correction: sprint every rep
3. Losing focus after a miss streak → correction: emphasize process (mechanics) over make count

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Workout #11-10 (10 makes per spot)
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #11-11 (20 makes per spot)
3. **Advanced**: Workout #11-12 (30 makes per spot) → then transition to [[drill-weakest-spot-shooting-workouts]]
