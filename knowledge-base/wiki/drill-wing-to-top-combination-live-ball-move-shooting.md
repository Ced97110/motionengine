# Wing to Top Combination Live-Ball Move Shooting (Workout #11-17)

## Objective
Develop the ability to execute combination live-ball moves (jab step, shot fake, step back) from beyond the three-point line while working through the wing-to-top-to-wing movement pattern at game speed.

## Setup
- 1 player (rebounder/passer recommended)
- Half court, three-point arc
- Same 3-spot structure as [[drill-wing-to-top-three-point-shooting]]: right wing, top of key, left wing
- Shot chart tracking 3PTA, 3PTM, %, FTA, FTM, %
- **Total shots**: 80 per full session (60 field goal attempts + 20 free throws)

## Execution
1. Player moves from **right wing** toward the **top of the key** and back to the **wing** — same wing-to-top movement pattern as Workout #11-16.
2. At each spot, the player **catches on a two-foot stop** past the three-point line.
3. Upon catching, the player executes a **combination live-ball move** (e.g., jab-step-and-shoot, jab-jab-jumper, shot-fake-and-shoot, or step-back) before firing the shot.
4. Repeat until **10 shots** have been taken per set.
5. Full session structure:
   - Right Side: Combo Shots (10) → FTs (5) → Combo Shots (10) → FTs (5) → Combo Shots (10)
   - Left Side: same structure
6. All shots and moves must be executed at **game speed**.

## Coaching Points
- The **two-foot stop** is mandatory — establishes a live-ball position from which either pivot foot is available.
- Combination moves must look game-realistic — "Sell the fake before you shoot."
- Don't rush the move into the shot; the move creates space, so let it work before rising.
- Track FGA/FGM/% to hold players accountable to a 60% field goal benchmark.
- Interleave **5 free throws between sets** — free throws after physical reps simulate late-game pressure.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Call out the move type before each rep so the player knows what to execute (removes decision-making initially).
2. **Intermediate**: Player chooses any combination move based on feel — builds decision-making.
3. **Advanced**: Add a live closeout defender; player must read the defender and execute the appropriate move.
