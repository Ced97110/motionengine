# Star Drill — Sprint, Bear Crawl, Shuffle

## Objective
Improve ability to change direction, body position, and transition between sprint, bear crawl, and lateral shuffle in all four diagonal directions.

## Setup
- 1 player
- 5 cones in a 10-yard square with one cone in the center (identical to Star Drill #72 setup)

## Execution
1. Start at cone 1.
2. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 1 to cone 5.
3. **Bear crawl diagonally** from cone 5 back to cone 1.
4. **Shuffle laterally** from cone 1 to cone 4.
5. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 4 to cone 5.
6. **Bear crawl** from cone 5 back to cone 4.
7. **Shuffle** from cone 4 to cone 3.
8. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 3 to cone 5.
9. **Bear crawl** from cone 5 back to cone 3.
10. **Shuffle** from cone 3 to cone 2.
11. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 2 to cone 5.
12. **Bear crawl** from cone 5 back to cone 2.
13. **Shuffle** from cone 2 to cone 1.

## Coaching Points
- Keep hips low and back flat during bear crawl; do not let hips rise or pike
- Shuffle segments: do not cross feet; stay parallel to the cone-line facing out
- Transition from bear crawl to sprint/shuffle is the critical moment — train explosive hip extension
- "Floor to fast" — be the quickest off the ground coming out of the bear crawl

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Remove bear crawl; practice sprint + shuffle star only.
2. **Intermediate**: Full star pattern as described.
3. **Advanced (Complex Variation)**: Replace shuffles with backpedals for an additional challenge [S8, p.100].
