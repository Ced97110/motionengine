# Star Drill — Sprint, Backpedal, Shuffle

## Objective
Improve ability to change direction, body position, and transition between sprint, backpedal, and lateral shuffle in all directions.

## Setup
- 1 player
- 5 cones in a 10-yard square with one cone in the center:
  - Cone 1: start (bottom right)
  - Cone 2: top right
  - Cone 3: top left
  - Cone 4: bottom left
  - Cone 5: center of the square

<!-- DIAGRAM: 10×10 yard square with center cone. Player starts at cone 1 (bottom right). Arrows show diagonal sprint to cone 5, backpedal back, lateral shuffle around perimeter. See [S8, p.98] -->

## Execution
1. Start at cone 1 in a ready position.
2. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 1 to cone 5 (center).
3. **Backpedal diagonally** from cone 5 back to cone 1.
4. **Shuffle laterally** from cone 1 to cone 4.
5. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 4 to cone 5.
6. **Backpedal** from cone 5 to cone 4.
7. **Shuffle laterally** from cone 4 to cone 3.
8. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 3 to cone 5.
9. **Backpedal** from cone 5 to cone 3.
10. **Shuffle laterally** from cone 3 to cone 2.
11. **Sprint diagonally** from cone 2 to cone 5.
12. **Backpedal** from cone 5 to cone 2.
13. **Shuffle laterally** from cone 2 to cone 1.

## Coaching Points
- Stay low and in an athletic stance throughout the entire drill
- Do not cross feet during the shuffle segments
- Explode off the backpedal transition — lean hips forward to accelerate
- Eyes forward at all times, not looking at the ground
- "Change your speed, not just your direction" — every sprint should be maximum effort

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through the full pattern to memorize the sequence before adding speed.
2. **Intermediate**: Game-speed execution; time the full circuit.
3. **Advanced**: Repeat 3–5 times with minimal rest; add a visual/auditory start signal.
