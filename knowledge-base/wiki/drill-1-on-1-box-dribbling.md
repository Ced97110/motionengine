# 1-on-1 in the Box (Workout #7-2)

## Objective
Apply the four key dribble moves (strong dribble, rocker, rocker-to-crossover, switch dribble) against a live, full-pressure defender in a confined space. [S3, p.173]

## Setup
- 2 players: 1 offensive, 1 defensive
- Top of the key — imaginary box (approximately free-throw line width, 2–3 ft deep)
- No equipment needed
- Coach sets the time interval

## Execution
1. Both players position themselves inside the imaginary box at the top of the key.
2. Offensive player works through the four dribble moves while the **defender applies maximum pressure**:
   - **Strong dribble**: low, hard, protected dribble
   - **Rocker dribble**: forward/backward hesitation dribble
   - **Rocker-to-crossover dribble**: rocker combined with a front crossover change of direction
   - **Switch dribble**: smooth hand transfer under pressure
3. **Defender is required to go hard** — the goal is to create as much ball pressure as legally possible.
4. Both players must stay in the box — if either steps out, reset and continue.
5. Progress through time intervals:
   - 15 seconds
   - 30 seconds
   - 45 seconds
   - 60 seconds

## Coaching Points
- **Defense must be maximum effort**: a lazy defender defeats the purpose — the offense must feel true game pressure.
- **Stay in the box**: confinement is the constraint that develops real handle; stepping out of the box is an escape valve that must be removed.
- **Offensive player: protect the ball**: use the body as a shield, keep the dribble low, and keep eyes up to read the defender.
- **Switch with purpose**: the switch dribble is not a casual crossover — it must be done low and quickly to avoid deflection.
- Coaching cue: *"Defense: take it. Offense: protect it. Neither one leaves the box."*

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Start with [[drill-1-on-0-box-dribbling]] to build move fluency before adding live defense.
2. **Intermediate**: 15-30 second intervals with live defense.
3. **Advanced**: 60-second full pressure; transition directly into [[drill-open-court-dribble-moves]] when the coach says "Go".
