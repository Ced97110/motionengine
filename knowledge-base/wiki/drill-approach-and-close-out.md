# Approach and Close Out Drill (#111)

## Objective
Teach defenders to close out on a live dribbler (not a stationary receiver) and contain the ball handler before competing 1-on-1.

## Setup
- **Players**: Two lines at opposite wings (attacker 1, defender X1 per station); multiple groups run simultaneously at each wing
- **Court area**: Half court, both wing positions
- **Rotation**: Offense → Defense → End of line; first player in line becomes next attacker
- **Equipment**: 1 basketball per station

<!-- DIAGRAM: Two simultaneous stations at left and right wings. X1 rolls ball forward to 1 who attacks; X1 must close out and contain. Waiting line behind each X1 position. -->

## Execution
1. Players line up in two lines (one per wing); rotation is offense → defense → end of line.
2. X1 rolls the ball to 1. Player 1 **immediately fakes and drives** upon receiving the rolling ball.
3. X1 must **close out on the dribbler** and bring the dribbler under control.
4. Once X1 has closed out and contained the ball, X1 and 1 play 1-on-1 to completion.
5. **Option**: X1 rolls the ball to 1. X1 must get to 1 and close out on the **pass receiver** before 1 begins a drive (simulates pass-and-close-out scenario rather than roll-and-close-out).

## Coaching Points
- Closing out on a live dribbler is harder than closing out on a stationary receiver — X1 must sprint at an angle to cut off the drive lane
- X1's goal: slow the ball handler down, force a direction, contain — not steal the ball
- "Bring the dribbler under control" before engaging 1v1 — rushing into the attacker is a foul
- Watch for X1 stopping too far away; must get close enough to affect the shot without being beaten on the drive
- The option variation (close out on a pass receiver) is more game-realistic; progress to this version after mastering the roll version

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: X1 rolls the ball to 1; close out on the dribbler
2. **Intermediate**: X1 rolls the ball; close out before 1 begins a drive (pass-receiver version)
3. **Advanced**: Increase the distance X1 must close out from; add live 1v1 competition with score kept
