# 360 Jump

## Objective
Develop lower-body explosive power, rotational body awareness (kinesthesia), and balanced landings by jumping and spinning a full 360° to land back in the starting position.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Any flat surface with adequate clearance
- No equipment required

## Execution
1. Stand in athletic stance.
2. Jump vertically with maximum effort.
3. While airborne, spin the body a full 360°.
4. Land back approximately where the jump began, in a balanced athletic stance.
5. Repeat for desired reps.
6. Practice both clockwise and counter-clockwise directions.

## Coaching Points
- "Fast hands and fast feet go together" — arm drive initiates the spin
- Always master both directions — clockwise AND counter-clockwise
- Land softly with bent knees — use the stretch-shortening cycle for immediate rebound
- Progress from 180° before attempting 360°

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: 180° jump (turn to face opposite direction) — land balanced, then progress
2. **Intermediate**: Full 360° with pause between reps
3. **Advanced**: Consecutive 360° jumps with minimal ground contact time
