# Dribble Curl Shot Drill Series

## Objective
Train the cutter to recognize when the defender is trailing directly behind on a curl cut and to continue the curl momentum with a dribble into the middle of the paint for any scoring opportunity.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter + passer (+ stationary screener or cone)
- **Court area**: Elbow and paint, both sides of the floor
- **Equipment**: Stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Screener position**: Near the three-second lane
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start**: Underneath the basket

## Execution
1. Shooter starts underneath the basket and approaches the stationary screener.
2. Shooter curls tightly around the screen toward the elbow area.
3. The simulated defender is **directly behind the cutter** (trailing the curl), not to the side — meaning the elbow shot is contested.
4. Instead of stopping to shoot, the cutter **dribble curls to the middle** of the paint, continuing the cut momentum directly into a live-ball attack.
5. Shooter uses **any available scoring opportunity**: floater, layup, pull-up, or draw the foul.
6. Shoot **5 free throws** after each dribble curl set.
7. In the 100-rep version, alternate left and right sides of the floor.

## Coaching Points
- **"If they're directly behind you, don't stop — keep attacking"** — this is the key read distinction from the curl shot
- Emphasize that this is an **extension of the curl momentum**, not a reset from a catch position
- Watch for players who stop at the elbow and then try to dribble — reinforce "continuous motion, not stop-and-start"
- **"Attack the middle, not the baseline"** — the trailing defender is behind, so middle is the open direction
- Coach the distinction between dribble curl (continuing the curl attack) vs. dribble fill (reading from a caught-ball position): "dribble curl has more momentum" [S3, p.171]
- Hold follow-through on any pull-up or mid-range finish inside

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-16)**: Single side, 5 dribble curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets. [S3, p.171]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-17)**: Single side. Target: 15/25 curls (60%), 21/25 FTs (84%). [S3, p.172]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-18)**: Both sides, 5 dribble curls + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets each; or 10 dribble curls + 5 FTs per side × 5 sets. Total 150 shots. Target: 60/100 curls (60%), 43/50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.173-179]
