# Combinations in the Clutch (Workout #4-25)

## Objective
Test a player's full offensive combination skill set in a high-pressure, point-scored workout that simulates clutch game conditions across the entire spectrum of live-ball moves.

## Setup
- 1 player (with optional rebounder/passer)
- Full basket access (half court)
- 1 basketball
- Score sheet to track points
- **Total shots**: 130 (80 FG, 18 3PT, 32 FT)
- **Total possible points**: 148 (FG=1pt, FT=1pt, 3PT=2pts each)
- **Clutch threshold**: 97 points [S3]

## Execution

**Scoring system**: Each field goal = 1 point | Each free throw = 1 point | Each 3-pointer = 2 points

**Sequence** (10 shots + 4 FTs per segment):
1. Combo 1 Dribble Stride Stop × 10, then FTs × 4
2. Combo 2 Dribble Stride Stop × 10, then FTs × 4
3. Combo 1 or 2 Dribble Big Hop × 10, then FTs × 4
4. Combo 1 Dribble Hop Back 3s × 10, then FTs × 4
5. Combo 2 Dribble Hop Back × 10, then FTs × 4
6. Combo 1 Dribble Hop Back to Roll Out Dribble × 10, then FTs × 4
7. Combo 2 Dribble Hop Back to Roll Out Dribble × 10, then FTs × 4
8. Combo 1 Dribble Hop Back to Crossover Dribble × 10, then FTs × 4
9. Combo 2 Dribble Hop Back to Crossover Dribble × 10
10. Game Speed 3PT Shots × 8 [S3]

**Scoring accountability rule**: If the player scores fewer than 97 points, they must shoot one additional free throw for every point below 97. (Example: 92 points → shoot 5 extra free throws.) Coaches may adjust the 97-point threshold up or down to match the player's ability level. [S3]

**3PT restriction**: Players are allowed a maximum of 18 three-point attempts total. Must alternate directions on every shot.

## Coaching Points
- "This is not practice — this is game day in the gym. Play with a score on the board."
- Track total points after every segment to maintain pressure and awareness
- The clutch threshold (97 pts) can be scaled: too easy → raise it; too hard → lower it
- Alternate attack directions on every shot to prevent habit formation
- Remind players the penalty rule is real — sloppy shooting has consequences [S3]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Lower the clutch threshold to 75 points; reduce segment reps by half
2. **Intermediate**: Use the standard 97-point threshold with full reps
3. **Advanced**: Raise threshold to 110+ points; add a live defender on selected segments
