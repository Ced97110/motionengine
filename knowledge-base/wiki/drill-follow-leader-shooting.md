# Follow-the-Leader Shooting Series (Workouts #11-4, #11-5, #11-6, #11-7, #11-8, #11-9)

## Objective
Develop competitive shooting repetition and accountability by having each player mirror the exact shot, location, and move chosen by the player in front of them — building consistency, imitation, and game-speed shooting habits.

## Setup
- 2–20 players, 1 ball per pair or group
- Half court or full court depending on player count
- No equipment beyond the ball
- All shots are self-pass spinouts (throw ball out from under the basket)

## Execution

### Universal Structure (All Follow-the-Leader Variants)
1. Player 1 throws the ball out toward the center of the court from under the basket.
2. Player 1 sprints to the ball, performs the designated move (or simply squares up), and shoots.
3. Player 2 immediately does the **exact same thing** — same spot, same move.
4. While Player 2 shoots, Player 1 hustles after the rebound and prepares for the next rep.
5. After each set of 10 shots per player, everyone shoots 5 free throws before the next set.
6. Repeat for 4 sets (40 field goal attempts + 20 FTs total per player).

### Workout #11-4: Follow-the-Leader Three-Point Shots
- All shots must be three-point attempts
- 4 sets × 10 three-point shots + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** 3PT 24–40 (60%); FTs 17–20 (85%); Total 60 shots

### Workout #11-5: Follow-the-Leader Combination Live-Ball Move Shots
- Player 1 chooses any combination live-ball move (jab-and-go, step-back, show-and-go, etc.)
- Player 2 copies the exact same move and shot
- 4 sets × 10 combo live-ball move shots + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** FG 24–40 (60%); FTs 17–20 (85%)

### Workout #11-6: Follow-the-Leader Rip Step Shots
- Player 1 chooses a rip step live-ball move of their choice, then shoots
- Player 2 copies the exact same rip step and shot
- 4 sets × 10 rip step shots + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** FG 24–40 (60%); FTs 17–20 (85%)

### Workout #11-7: Follow-the-Leader Shot Fake (SF) Shots
- Player 1 throws ball out, performs a shot fake (SF) into any scoring opportunity, and shoots
- Player 2 copies the exact same shot fake sequence and finish
- 4 sets × 10 shot fake shots + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** FG 24–40 (60%); FTs 17–20 (85%)

### Workout #11-8: Follow-the-Leader Spot Shots
- Player 1 chooses any spot-shot location
- Player 2 copies exact spot and shooting technique
- 4 sets × 10 spot shots + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** same as above

### Workout #11-9: Follow-the-Leader Open-Court Dribble Moves
- Player 1 chooses an open-court dribble move (crossover, between legs, behind back, etc.) into a scoring opportunity
- Player 2 copies the exact same dribble move sequence and shot
- 4 sets × 10 shots off open-court dribble move + 5 FTs between each set
- **Target:** FG 24–40 (60%); FTs 17–20 (85%)

## Coaching Points
- "Copy the leader exactly — same spot, same move, same finish."
- First player should sprint after rebounding; never walk to conserve time and energy.
- Encourage players to choose realistic shots from their offensive arsenal, not just easy buckets.
- Track makes and attempts on a shot chart after each workout.
- Free throws between sets are not a break — maintain the same focus and routine.
- This drill is scalable: with 20 players, the line moves quickly and reps stay high for everyone.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (Workout #11-8):** Spot shots only — no move required; focus on catch-and-shoot mechanics.
2. **Intermediate (Workouts #11-4, #11-6):** Add three-point distance or rip step live-ball moves.
3. **Advanced (Workouts #11-5, #11-7, #11-9):** Full combination moves, shot fakes, and open-court dribble sequences at game speed.
