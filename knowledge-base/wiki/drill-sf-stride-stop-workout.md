# Shot Fake (SF) Stride Stop Shooting Workouts

## Objective
Develop a complete and ambidextrous shot-fake-into-stride-stop pull-up jump shot by training all four direction/dribble combinations at progressive volumes with built-in free throw pressure.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court, midrange areas (approximately elbow, wing, short corner)
- No additional equipment needed
- Shot chart available for tracking (FGA/FGM, FTA/FTM)

## Execution

### Workout #3-6 (240 total shots — advanced volume)
1. SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop **Left** × 20 shots
2. Free throws × 10
3. SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop **Right** × 20 shots
4. Free throws × 10
5. SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop **Left** × 20 shots
6. Free throws × 10
7. SF 1-Dribble Stride Stop **Right** × 20 shots
8. Free throws × 10
9. SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop **Left** × 20 shots
10. Free throws × 10
11. SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop **Right** × 20 shots
12. Free throws × 10
13. SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop **Left** × 20 shots
14. Free throws × 10
15. SF 2-Dribble Stride Stop **Right** × 20 shots
16. Free throws × 10

**Targets:** 97-160 FG (60%), 68-80 FT (85%) [S3]

### Execution per rep:
1. Start in triple-threat position with ball.
2. Show a convincing upward shot fake — ball rises to shot pocket, eyes on rim.
3. When defender (or imagined defender) reacts, immediately attack with 1 or 2 hard dribbles in the designated direction.
4. Execute a controlled stride stop: non-shooting foot lands first, then shooting foot.
5. Jump straight up into a balanced pull-up jump shot.
6. Hold follow-through until ball hits the floor.
7. Retrieve ball, reset, repeat.

## Coaching Points
- "Make the fake real — if you wouldn't shoot it, your defender won't bite."
- Attack within one split-second of the fake; hesitation gives the defense time to recover.
- The stride stop foot sequence is non-shooting foot → shooting foot; do NOT jump-stop.
- Both feet should be pointed at the basket on the stop — avoid sideways drift.
- Free throws between sets are mandatory — they simulate composure under fatigue.
- Track every shot on the chart: date, FGA, FGM, %, FTA, FTM, %.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Remove from workout rotation; start with [[drill-show-and-go-closeout-1on1]] and basic [[drill-pull-up-jump-shot]] first.
2. **Intermediate (Workout #3-6 structure)**: Use 20-shot sets with full 60% FG target at game pace.
3. **Advanced**: Add a live defender in the closeout position reading the real fake/drive decision.
