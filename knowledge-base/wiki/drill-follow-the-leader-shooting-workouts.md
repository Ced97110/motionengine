# Follow the Leader Shooting Workouts

## Objective
Develop shot consistency, competitive accountability, and full-speed shooting habits by having players mirror each other's exact shot — including spot location, move, and scoring action.

## Setup
- 2 to 20 players, 1 ball per pair (or group takes turns)
- Full court or half court depending on group size
- All shots from outside 5 feet; three-point shots permitted
- Each set ends with 5 free throws by each player

## Execution
1. Player 1 throws the ball out toward the center of the court and takes a shot (spot shot, three-pointer, or live-ball move — depending on the workout variant).
2. Player 2 hustles to the same location and replicates the **exact same shot** — same spot, same move.
3. While Player 2 is shooting, Player 1 sprints after the rebound and gets ready for the next rep.
4. The players alternate in this manner for the full set.
5. After each set of 10 shots per player, both players shoot 5 free throws.
6. Repeat for 4 sets (40 total field goal attempts + 20 FTs per player).

## Workout Variants

### Workout #11-4: Follow the Leader Three-Point Shots
All shots are three-pointers from the spot chosen by Player 1. [S3, pp.203-204]

| Set | Shot Type | Count |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| 1–4 | 3PT Spot Shot | 10 each |
| 1–4 | FTs | 5 each |

**Total:** 40 3PT attempts + 20 FTs
**Targets:** 3PT 24–40 (60%); FT 17–20 (85%)

### Workout #11-5: Follow the Leader Combination Live-Ball Move Shots
Player 1 chooses any combination live-ball move (e.g., jab-step into shot, show-and-go, step-back) and Player 2 replicates. [S3, pp.205-206]

| Set | Shot Type | Count |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| 1–4 | Combination Live-Ball Move | 10 each |
| 1–4 | FTs | 5 each |

**Total:** 40 FGA + 20 FTs
**Targets:** FG 24–40 (60%); FT 17–20 (85%)

### Workout #11-6: Follow the Leader Rip Step Shots

## Coaching Points
- **Mirror exactly** — spot location, move choice, and footwork must be identical. This builds communication and competitive awareness.
- **Sprint after every rebound** — no walking. This is a conditioning drill as much as a shooting drill.
- **Player 1 sets the standard** — challenge leaders to choose their best shots, not the easiest.
- **Free throws are mandatory** — stepping to the line after exertion trains the mental and physical routine needed in late-game situations.
- Track FGA, FGM, %, FTA, FTM, % by date to measure long-term improvement.
- **Sample elite result (8/7):** 40 FGA/28 FGM (70%), 20 FTA/19 FTM (95%) [S3, p.205]

## Common Mistakes
1. Walking after rebounds → correction: demand sprint every single rep
2. Player 2 choosing an easier location → correction: must match Player 1's exact spot
3. Skipping free throws → correction: FTs are non-negotiable between sets
4. Shooting inside 5 feet → correction: outside shots only in all variants

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Workout #11-8 (spot shots only — no live-ball moves)
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #11-4 (three-point shots) or #11-7 (shot fakes)
3. **Advanced**: Workout #11-5 (combination moves) or #11-9 (open-court dribble moves)
