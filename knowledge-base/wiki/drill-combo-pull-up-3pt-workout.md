# Combination Live-Ball Pull-Up 3-Point Workout Series

## Objective
Develop the ability to create and shoot a pull-up three-pointer off live-ball combination moves, with high-volume reps alternating directions and free throws between sets.

## Setup
- 1 player
- Three-point line and full basket access
- 1 basketball
- Optional rebounder/passer

## Execution

### Workout #4-22 (25 Pull-Up 3s)
- Combination pull-up 3-pointers: 5 sets × 5 shots = 25 total 3PT attempts
- Free throws between each set: 5 sets × 5 FTs = 25 total FTs [S3]

### Workout #4-23 (50 Pull-Up 3s)
- Combination pull-up 3-pointers: 5 sets × 10 shots = 50 total 3PT attempts
- Free throws between each set: 5 sets × 10 FTs = 50 total FTs
- **Targets**: 3PT 15-25 (60%), FT 21-25 (85%) [S3]

### Workout #4-24 (100 Pull-Up 3s)
- Combination pull-up 3-pointers: 10 sets × 10 shots = 100 total 3PT attempts
- Free throws between each set: 10 sets × 10 FTs = 100 total FTs
- **Targets**: 3PT 30-50 (60%), FT 43-50 (85%) [S3]

**Execution mechanics for all workouts:**
1. Start with the ball live (dribbling)
2. Execute a combination move (hop-back, crossover, stride stop, big hop) to create space
3. Gather and fire a pull-up three-pointer
4. Alternate directions on each attempt
5. Shoot free throws between sets without rest — builds FT routine under light fatigue

## Coaching Points
- The three-pointer must come off a live-ball combination move — not a standing catch-and-shoot
- "Find your rhythm off the dribble — the gather is part of the shot"
- Track 3PT attempts and makes separately from FTs; target 60% from three off combos
- Alternate left and right attack directions every rep to develop two-way threat
- At the Workout #4-24 level, fatigue management is a training goal — maintain technique through all 100 reps [S3]

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Workout #4-22 (25 reps) — low volume, focus on correct mechanics
2. **Intermediate**: Workout #4-23 (50 reps) — sustained output at 60% target
3. **Advanced**: Workout #4-24 (100 reps) — full training volume, game-speed combos
