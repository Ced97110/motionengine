# Dribble Fade Shooting Drill

## Objective
Train the cutter to read a high-angle closeout from a defender who has slipped the screen, then attack the baseline with a dribble fade and convert any scoring opportunity.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter (cutter), passer, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Court area**: Half court — wing, elbow, baseline
- **Screener position**: On the wing or at the elbow, simulating a down screen or wing screen
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Equipment**: Basketballs, optional cones/chairs as screener substitute

## Execution
1. Shooter begins underneath the basket and V-cuts to set up the screen
2. Shooter uses the stationary screener to get open on the wing
3. Passer delivers the ball to the shooter coming off the screen
4. The drill simulates the defender slipping the screen and closing out at a **high angle**, leaving the baseline open
5. Shooter catches the ball on the wing and immediately establishes a dominant pivot foot
6. Shooter attacks the baseline with a dribble fade
7. Any scoring opportunity is taken once the baseline is attacked — fade-away jumper, floater, layup, or pull-up
8. After the scoring attempt, shooter shoots 5 free throws, then repeats on the same side or switches sides

## Coaching Points
- "Read the defender before the ball arrives — if they close high, the baseline is yours"
- Establish the dominant pivot foot instantly on the catch — do not catch flat-footed
- The dribble fade is not just a retreat — it is an attack of the open baseline space
- Keep the dribble low and controlled when attacking the baseline
- Any scoring opportunity is valid: fade-away jumper, one-dribble pull-up, layup, or floater
- Free throws between sets simulate the physical and mental demands of game free throws after contact

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-5)**: 5 dribble fades + 5 FTs, alternating × 5 sets. Targets: 15–25 fades made (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). Work one side of the floor. [S3, p.141]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-5)**: 15–25 fades (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). Add live defender pressure or increase dribble speed. [S3, p.141]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-6)**: Split evenly — left side and right side of floor (5 dribble fades + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side). Targets: 30–50 fades (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.141-143]
4. **Elite (150 shots — continuation of Workout #5-6)**: 10 dribble fades + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 60–100 fades (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.143]
