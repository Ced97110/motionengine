# Zigzag On-Ball Pressure Drill (Fratello)

## Objective
Build full-court on-ball defensive footwork by having the defender step-and-slide to repeatedly cut off the dribbler from both the lane and the sideline.

## Setup
- 2 players per group (1 offense, 1 defense)
- Full court, starting at one corner outside the baseline
- Run simultaneously on both sides of the court if roster allows

## Execution
1. **Part 1 — Stationary Slide:** Offensive player dribbles hard toward the lane; defensive player steps and slides to cut off the lane. Offensive player reverses and dribbles hard to the sideline; defender steps and slides to cut off the sideline. Continue this oscillation (lane → sideline → lane) until reaching half-court. Players exchange roles.
2. **Part 2 — Jab and Reverse:** Same setup. Now the dribbler takes two hard dribbles and picks up the ball. Defensive player raises both hands with wrists crossed to "jam" and mirror the ball, preventing the pass. Dribbler picks up the ball 3–4 times; defender jams each time. Players exchange roles.
3. **Part 3 — Live to Half:** Offensive player now plays normally, trying to beat the defender to half-court. Defender steps and slides and cuts the dribbler off as in a game. At half-court, roles are exchanged and players return via the same method.

## Coaching Points
- Defender stays in a wide, athletic defensive stance — never stand upright
- "Step and slide" — do not cross feet; lead with the foot in the direction of the dribbler
- On jam: wrists crossed, hands up, no lower than shoulders
- "Cut off the lane first; the sideline is your wall"
- Do NOT reach — mirror and jam with the body, not the hands
- The goal of Part 3 is to force a speed dribble, NOT to steal the ball

## Progressions
1. **Beginner:** Walk through the step-and-slide footwork without the ball
2. **Intermediate:** Part 1 and 2 as described
3. **Advanced:** Part 3 — live to half-court; add scoring if dribbler beats defender clean
