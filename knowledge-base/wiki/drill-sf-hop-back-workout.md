# Shot Fake (SF) Hop Back Workouts

## Objective
Develop the ability to create an open three-pointer (or midrange) by using a shot fake to drive a defender forward, then hopping backward to step behind the arc and shoot.

## Setup
- 1 player, 1 ball
- Half court, starting from midrange — hop-back lands behind 3-point arc
- Shot chart for tracking (FGA/FGM, 3PTA/3PTM, FTA/FTM)

## Execution

### Workout #3-13 (40 total shots — introductory)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Left** × 5 | FTs × 5
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Right** × 5 | FTs × 5

**Targets:** 6-10 FG (60%), 6-10 3PT (60%), 17-20 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-14 (80 total shots)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Left** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Right** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Left** × 10 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Right** × 10 | FTs × 10

**Targets:** 12-20 FG (60%), 12-20 3PT (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Workout #3-15 (120 total shots)
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Left** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 1-Dribble Hop Back 3s **Right** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Left** × 20 | FTs × 10
- SF 2-Dribble Hop Back **Right** × 20 | FTs × 10

**Targets:** 24-40 FG (60%), 24-40 3PT (60%), 34-40 FT (85%) [S3]

### Per Rep Execution:
1. Start in triple-threat position with feet just inside the three-point line.
2. Execute convincing shot fake.
3. Take 1 or 2 explosive attack dribbles toward the basket.
4. Explode BACKWARD off both feet simultaneously — hop landing must be behind the three-point arc.
5. Land balanced with knees bent, catch-and-shoot immediately.
6. Freeze follow-through.

## Coaching Points
- "Drive them into you — then hop out."
- The 1-dribble hop back must land BEHIND the three-point line; practice the exact distance.
- Land with balanced two-foot base, NOT leaning backward — backward lean creates airballs.
- Quick release — the space created by the hop back is temporary.
- 3-point tracking (3PTA/3PTM) in addition to FG% is included on the workout chart.
- Don't skip the FTs — hop-back 3s are often drawn in late-game situations under pressure.

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Practice the backward hop from a stationary position first. No dribble — just hop back from midrange and shoot the three.
2. **Intermediate**: Add 1 attack dribble (Workout #3-13 volume — 5 reps).
3. **Advanced**: Full Workout #3-15 (20 reps × 4 sets) at game speed.
