# Piston Elevator

## Overview
A 1-4 high half-court play that disguises itself as an Iverson wing exchange but uses both post players to set an elevator screen, freeing the best shooter for a top-of-key catch-and-shoot three-pointer. The decoy Iverson cut by 3 draws defensive attention while 2 sneaks through the elevator. [S4, pp.68-69]

## Formation
1-4 high: 1 at the top with the ball, 4 and 5 on the elbows, 2 on the right wing, 3 on the left wing. [S4, p.68]

## Phases

### Phase 1: Decoy Iverson Cut & Baseline Cut
- 1 dribbles toward one side of the court.
- 3 makes an Iverson cut off the elbow screens set by 4 and 5 — this is the decoy to draw defensive attention.
- Simultaneously, 2 makes a baseline cut to the middle of the key. 2 should wait an extra moment before starting the baseline cut to allow the elevator screen to be set in time. [S4, p.68]

### Phase 2: Elevator Screen
- Immediately after 4 and 5 set the Iverson elbow screens for 3, they quickly pivot and form an **elevator screen** (two screeners standing side-by-side with a gap, then closing together after the cutter passes through).
- 2 bursts through the screens to the top of the key and receives the pass from 1 for the catch-and-shoot three-pointer. [S4, p.68]
- 4 and 5 must **"close the gate"** after 2 has cleared through to screen x2. [S4, p.69]

### Phase 3: Counter — Dive to Rim
- If x4 anticipates the pass and cuts off the passing lane to 2, 4 dives hard to the hoop for the layup. [S4, p.69]

## Key Coaching Points
- 1 must **not** dribble toward the better shooter — the player 1 dribbles at always goes over the screens (the Iverson cutter). 1 must dribble away from 2. [S4, p.68]
- 2 must make a **quick change of direction** in the key and explode through the elevator. No slowing down. 2 should delay their baseline cut slightly so the elevator closes at the right time. [S4, p.68]
- 4 and 5 must close the gate (step together) immediately after 2 cuts through to seal x2 out. [S4, p.69]
- If x4 predicts and jumps the pass, 4 dives to the rim instead. [S4, p.69]

## Key Personnel
- **1 (PG)**: Dribble signal determines which player goes over the screens; must deliver the pass on-time to 2.
- **2 (SG / Best Shooter)**: The elevator cutter; must be patient, change direction explosively, and be ready to shoot immediately.
- **3 (SF)**: Decoy Iverson cutter; draws defensive attention away from the elevator.
- **4 (PF)**: Sets Iverson elbow screen, then pivots to form the elevator; closes the gate after 2 cuts through.
- **5 (C)**: Sets Iverson elbow screen, then pivots to form the elevator; closes the gate after 2 cuts through.

## Counters
- If x4 cuts off the pass to 2, 4 dives to the rim for the layup.
- If the defense switches, 5 can slip or seal for a post-up.
