# In-and-Out Shuffle With Reaction (Quick Feet)

## Objective
Improve mental processing speed, court sense, total-body reaction time to visual and audible stimuli, total-body agility, and general athleticism. [S8, p.220]

## Setup
- 1 player, agility ladder
- Start in a **two-point stance facing one side** of the ladder
- Coach positioned at the side of or end of the ladder

## Execution
1. Stand in a two-point stance, **facing one side** of the ladder.
2. Step with the **left foot straight ahead** into the first square.
3. Follow with the **right foot** into the same square.
4. Step **back and diagonally with the left foot** until it is in front of the second square.
5. Follow with the **right foot** until it is in front of the second square.
6. Repeat this sequence — in (both feet), out (both feet advance) — through the entire ladder, making sure each foot touches each box.
7. **Simultaneously**, react to the coach's visual commands throughout the drill.

## Coaching Points
- Each foot must hit each box — do not skip squares
- Keep the pattern rhythmic and accelerate gradually through the ladder
- Divide attention: footwork automatic, eyes tracking coach
- "In-in, out-out — hit every box, see every cue!"

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Learn the footwork pattern slowly without cues
2. **Intermediate**: Add coach finger-count visual reaction at game speed
3. **Advanced (Complex Variations)**:
   - Odd/even number filtering
   - Coach semicircle for peripheral vision
   - Multiple coaches around periphery [S8, p.220]
   - Sport-specific skill at end of ladder
