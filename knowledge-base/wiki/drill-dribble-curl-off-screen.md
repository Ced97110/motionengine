# Dribble Curl Off a Screen Drill

## Objective
Train the cutter to read a defender trailing directly behind the curl cut and continue attacking to the middle of the paint with a dribble curl, executing any scoring opportunity.

## Setup
- **Players**: Shooter (cutter), passer, stationary screener (player or cone/chair)
- **Court area**: Half court — elbow area and paint
- **Passer position**: Top of the key
- **Shooter start position**: Underneath the basket
- **Screener position**: Wing or near elbow

## Execution
1. Shooter begins underneath the basket and sets up the screen with a V-cut
2. Shooter curls tightly around the stationary screen toward the elbow area
3. Passer delivers the ball as the shooter curls past the screen
4. The drill simulates the defender **trailing the screen and positioned directly behind the cutter** — the cutter cannot pull up cleanly
5. Cutter establishes a dominant pivot foot on the catch and continues attacking to the **middle of the paint** with a dribble curl
6. Any scoring opportunity is executed once the middle has been attacked — floater, pull-up, layup, or pass out
7. After the scoring attempt, shoot 5 free throws, then repeat

## Coaching Points
- The dribble curl is similar to the dribble fill, but the cutter is **already attacking the basket** with a curl trajectory — the momentum is already toward the paint
- "Don't stop at the elbow — keep attacking if the defender is behind you"
- The curl cut's natural trajectory toward the basket is an advantage — use it
- Maintain low dribble control when driving from the elbow into the paint
- Any scoring opportunity is valid: floater, pull-up, reverse layup, or drawn foul
- Contrast with dribble fill: fill starts from a wider wing catch; curl starts from the elbow with the cutter already angled at the basket

## Progressions
1. **Beginner (25 shots — Workout #5-16)**: 5 dribble curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets. One side of floor. [S3, p.156]
2. **Intermediate (50 shots — Workout #5-17)**: 15–25 curls made (60%), 21–25 FTs (84%). One side. [S3, p.157]
3. **Advanced (100 shots — Workout #5-18)**: Split left/right — 5 dribble curls + 5 FTs × 5 sets per side. Targets: 30–50 curls (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, pp.157-158]
4. **Elite (150 shots — Workout #5-18 extended)**: 10 dribble curls + 5 FTs per set × 5 sets per side. Targets: 60–100 curls (60%), 43–50 FTs (86%). [S3, p.159]
