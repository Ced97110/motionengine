# Flex Offense Shooting Drill — Two Screens (4-on-4)

## Objective
Practice reading two simultaneous screens and choosing between a fade, curl, or roll-to-basket pass based on how defenders react — the most game-realistic flex shooting drill.

## Setup
- 4 offensive players vs. 4 defenders (half court)
- 1 with the ball at the top of the key
- 4 and 5 set simultaneous screens in the lane for 2
- Both screeners are live and can roll to the basket

## Execution
1. **1** has the ball at the top; **4 and 5** are positioned in the lane to set two screens for **2**
2. **2** decides which screen to use based on the move of their defender:
   - **If X2 tries to slide through the screen**: **5** changes the angle of the screen (re-screen), and **2 fades** away from the screen — creating enough space and time to receive and shoot (approximately 15-18 ft)
   - **If X2 follows behind 2**: **2 curls** around the screen, receives the ball, and shoots OR passes to **5 who has rolled to the basket** after setting the screen
   - **1 can also pass directly to 5 on the roll** if X5 sags to help on 2's curl
3. **1** reads the situation and passes to the best option: 2 on the fade, 2 on the curl, 4 rolling, or 5 rolling
4. Play continues live until an effective shooting situation cannot be found
5. Rotate offense and defense; track makes and stops

## Coaching Points
- "2, your cut is determined by where your defender goes — read, don't guess"
- Screeners (4 and 5): hold the screen until the defender commits, then roll hard to the rim
- "If they go through, we fade. If they follow, we curl and roll."
- 1 must keep eyes up and see all four options simultaneously
- On the re-screen: 5 must shift body angle subtly to create a new screening angle — do not step
- Defenders: practice communication — "through!" or "fight!" on every screen action

## Progressions
1. **Beginner**: Walk through both reads (fade and curl) without defenders; coach calls the read
2. **Intermediate**: Add one passive defender on 2 who either goes through or follows to trigger the correct read
3. **Advanced**: Full 4-on-4 live; track stops vs. makes over 5-minute segments
